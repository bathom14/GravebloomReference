**Prerequisites:** [[Half-Orc]], [[Orc]]

The strength of the Y’or-Ek runs through your veins. You gain the the following benefits.

* You gain advantage on Constitution saving throws.

* Your hit point maximum increases by an amount equal to your level when you gain this feat. Whenever you gain a level thereafter, your hit point maximum increased by an additional 1 hit points

* When an attacker that you can see hits you with an attack, you can see hits you with an attack, you can use your reaction to half that damage if it is bludgeoning, piercing, or slashing damage.
