*Prerequisite: Dragonborn*

You manifest scales and fangs reminiscent of your draconic ancestors. You gain the following benefits:

- Increase your Strength, Constitution, Intelligence, or Charisma score by 1, up to a maximum of 20.

- **Dragon Hide.** Your scales harden. While you aren't wearing armor, you can calculate your AC as 13 + your Constitution modifier. You can use a shield and still gain this benefit.

- **Draconic Fangs.** You can extend your draconic fangs at will. Extending or retracting the fangs requires no action. The fangs are natural weapons, which you can use to make unarmed strikes. If you hit with them, you deal piercing damage equal to 1d6 + your Constitution modifier, instead of the normal bludgeoning damage for an unarmed strike.
	- If you successfully kill a creature using your Draconic Fangs, you may recover one expended usage of your Breath Weapon.
