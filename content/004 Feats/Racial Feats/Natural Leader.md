**Prerequisites:** 4th level, [[Hobgoblin]]

You have been trained since birth to be a leader on the battlefield. You gain the following benefits.

* **Call Out.** As a reaction, you can utter a special command or warning whenever an ally you can see within 30 feet makes an attack roll or saving throw. This creature can add a d4 to the roll provided it can hear and understand you. A creature can only benefit from one such dice at a time. The dice increases as you level: a d6 at level 5, a d8 at level 11, and a d10 at level 17.

* **Capitalize.** Once per turn, you can deal an extra 2d6 damage to an enemy that you hit with a weapon attack if that creature is within 5 feet of an ally that isn’t incapacitated.
