**Prerequisites:** [[002 Races/Dwarf/index]]

You have advantage on saving throws against being frightened.
When fighting a creature that is larger than you, if you fail a saving throw you can instead choose to succeed. Once you use this ability, you must finish a long rest before you can do so again.
