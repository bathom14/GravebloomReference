**Prerequisites:** 4th level, [[Hill Dwarf]]

The Hill Dwarves are legendary for their prowess in artillery tactics and their fondness for overwhelming firepower. You receive the following benefits.

* **Big Guns.** Your Strength or Constitution score increases by 1. You count as one size larger when determining your carrying capacity and the weight you can push, drag, or lift.

* **Effecient Foundries.** You require half the material value to forge any Hand Cannons, Organ Guns, Cannons, or Mortars, as well as Cannonball and Bombshell ammunitions.

* **Battery Fire.** Whenever you use the Attack action with a Hand Cannon or fire a piece of Artillery using an action or bonus action, you may aim and fire a piece of Artillery as a free action. This can occur any number of times each turn. This may not trigger itself.

* **Quick Hands.** When operating Artillery pieces, you may perform any aim, load, and fire action at the cost of a single action, regardless of the size of the Artillery. For artillery of size Large or smaller, you may perform any Aim, Load, or fire action as a bonus action.

* **We're Gonna Need a Bigger Shell.** You may craft Cannons and Mortars of Gargantuan size. 
   - Their base weight modifier is 15552x, and their base cost modifier is 2592x.
      - The discount from Efficienct Foundries applies to the cost of these Gargantuan artillery pieces.
   - The Weight Class modifier is 1000x. The Range modifier is 2000x.
   - Gargantuan artillery pieces take 1 month of active work to complete.
   - The Gargantuan Cannon deals 32d12 blugeoning damage.
      - Gargantuan Cannonballs weigh 512lbs
   - The Gargantuan Mortar deals 16d12 blugeoning damage.
      - Gargantuan Bombshells weigh 384lbs
   - Gargantuan artillery pieces require 3 actions each to move, load, aim and fire.
