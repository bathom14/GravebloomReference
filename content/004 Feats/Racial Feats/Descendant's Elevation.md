*Prerequisite:* Dragonborn, Kobold, 4th Level

You manifest the signature wings of your antecessors. You gain the following benefits.
- **Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Strength, Constitution, Charisma, or Intelligence by 1.
- **Draconic Wings.** You sprout a pair of draconic wings. You gain a flying speed of 20 feet.
