**Prerequisites:** [[Aarakocra]]

- **Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Wisdom or Dexterity score by 1.
- **With the Wind.** You learn the [[Gust]] cantrip.
- **Like a Feather.** You learn the [[Feather Fall]] spell. You can cast this spell without expending a spell slot a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus. You regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest. You can also cast this spell using any spell slots you have of 1st level or higher.
	- Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma is your spellcasting modifier for this spell (choose when you select this feat.)
- **Tempest.** You learn the  [[Zephyr Strike]] spell. You can cast this spell without expending a spell slot a number of times equal to one-half of your proficiency bonus, rounded up. You regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest. You can also cast this spell using any spell slots you have of 1st level or higher.
	- Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma is your spellcasting modifier for this spell (choose when you select this feat.)
