**Prerequisites:** 12th level, [[High Elf]]

You have manifested one of the greatest blessings of Mai’ar. You gain the following benefits.

* **Identify.** Once per long rest, you may cast the [Dominate Monster](http://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:dominate-monster) spell without expending a spell slot, but it must be used to target a wyvern. If you target a wyvern this way and it has been dominated in the past, the spell fails but you regain your spent use.

* **Compell.** When you target a wyvern with the Dominate Monster spell, it has disadvantage on its saving throw.

* **Dominate.** If you charm a wyvern using Dominate Monster, you form a permanent psychic bond and dominate the wyvern. The wyvern's demeanor becomes friendly towards you, and it is permanently under the effect of Dominate Monster. It does not make any saving throws to end the effect. The wyvern is considered an ally, and considers you one, for as long as this spell is active. 

   - This is a passive effect. You do not need to maintain concentration.

   - This effect cannot be broken unless you dominate another wyvern, the wyvern dies or you die.

   - You may only have one wyvern bound to you in this way. If you dominate another wyvern, the effect ends on your currently controlled wyvern and it returns to its innate nature.
   - This wyvern will accommodate riders.
