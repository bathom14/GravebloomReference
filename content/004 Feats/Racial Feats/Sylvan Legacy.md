**Prerequisites:** 4th level, [[Wood Elf]]

You awaken some of the abilities of your Sylvan ancestors. You gain the following benefits.

* **Cloak.** You know the [Invisibility](http://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:invisibility) spell. You may cast it a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus without expending a spell slot, and you regain all spend uses when  you finish a long rest. Wisdom is your spellcasting modifier for this spell.

* **Stride.** You know the [Longstrider](http://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:longstrider) spell. You may cast this spell a number of time equal to your proficiency bonus without expending a spell slot, and you regain all spent uses when you finish a long rest. Wisdom is your spellcasting modifier for this spell.

* **Guard.** You know the [Guardian of Nature](http://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:guardian-of-nature) spell. You may cast this spell a number of times equal to one-half of your proficiency bonus, rounded down,  without expending a spell slot, and you regain all spend uses when you finish a long rest. Wisdom is your spellcasting modifier for this spell.
