**Prerequisites:** [[Hadozee]]

You are exceptionally skilled at gliding. You gain the following benefits

* **Running Start.** If you run at least 10 feet straight in one direction, you may use your bonus action to make a special jump of 15 feet into the air

* **Hang Time.** When gliding, you may keep yourself aloft temporarily. While you are gliding, you may choose to halt your fall after falling 5 feet. You are cosidered to be flying until your next turn. If your movespeed is reduced to 0, if you are knocked prone, or otherwise deprived of the ability of flight or movement you will immediately fall to the ground. Your fall resumes at the beginning of your next turn.

* **All Day.** When gliding, when you move at least 25 feet you may use your bonus action to cause an updraft which will elevate you 5 feet into the air. In a single glide, you may perform this updraft a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus.

* **Death From Above.** When you are gliding, you have advantage on attack rolls on creatures below yourself.
