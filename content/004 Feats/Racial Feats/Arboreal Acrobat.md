**Prerequisites:** [[Grung]]

You are particularly adept at navigating vertical environments. You gain the following benefits.

* **Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Dexterity or Constitution score by 1.

* **Sticky Pads.** You have the ability to scale vertical surfaces. If you end your turn on a vertical surface, you may remain attached to the wall in that position. While scaling vertical surface, you must keep at least 3 limbs in contact with the surface (one free hand)

* **Wall Jump.** When you are a attached to a vertical surface, you may make a jump of 25 feet in any direction, as long as that direction is away from the wall. When you make contact with a wall while jumping, you may choose to attach to that wall.

* **Light Fall.** When you would take damage from a fall, you can use your reaction to reduce the fall's damage to zero.
