**Prerequisites:** [[002 Races/Gnome]]

You become the spore mate of a common fungoid.

* **Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Wisdom or Charisma score by 1.

* **Friend.** The fungoid friend appears in the palm of your hand when you take this feat. It is initially friendly towards you and holds you in high esteem, and will generally follow your commands to the best of its ability. Due to it's great repect for you, it takes a name of your choice as its own.
