Your martial training has helped you develop a particular style of fighting. As a result, you gain the following benefits.

- **Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Strength, Dexterity, or Constitution by 1.

- **Fighting Style.** You are now considered to have the Fighting Style feature if you do not already have it, and you may choose one [[/004-Feats/Fighting-Style-Feats/|Fighting Style Feat]] of your choice that you qualify for. If you already have a fighting style feat, the one you choose must be different.

- **Martial Versatility.** Whenever you reach a level that grants the Ability Score Improvement feature, you can replace this feat's fighting style with another one from the fighter class that you don't have.
