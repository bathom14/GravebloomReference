**Prerequisite:** *Pact Magic or Spellcasting Feature*

- **Ability Score Increase.** Int or Cha by **1** (to a max of 20).

- **Arcane Distillation.** As a bonus action on your turn, expend one spell slot and gain a number of **Sorcery Points** equal to the slot’s level.

- **Arcane Synthesis.** As a bonus action on your turn, you can transform unexpended **Sorcery Points** into one spell slot using the table below. Any slot you create this way **vanishes when you finish a long rest**. A slot **created by this feat can’t be converted back into Sorcery Points**.


|Slot Level|Sorcery Point Cost|Minimum Spellcaster Level|
|---|---|---|
|1st|2|1st|
|2nd|3|3rd|
|3rd|5|5th|
|4th|6|7th|
|5th|7|9th|

_You may only create spell slots up to the level allowed by your **Spellcasting level** (multiclass rules)._
