title: Magical Overcast
note_type: feat

feat_race_req: []
feat_level_req: 
feat_feat_req:
---
When you cast a spell of 1st level or higher, you may choose to expend one additional spell slot to treat the casted spell as if it were cast using a spell slot equal to the slot it was cast from plus the expended spell slot. (For example, if you were to cast Fireball at 3rd level, you could expend a 2nd level slot when casting to cast it at 5th level.)
