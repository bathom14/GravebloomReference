# Origin Feat: [[Pathfinder]]

You are trained in the arts of navigation, surveying, and route planning, letting you move through and chart the world with ease.

- **Unerring Direction:** You always know the cardinal directions while above ground, and you can accurately estimate the time until sunrise or sunset without a check.

- **Surveyor’s Eye:** After spending 10 minutes observing an area, you can sketch a simple, to-scale map accurate enough for travel and basic tactical planning.

- **Route Planner:** When navigating, you can reduce travel penalties from difficult terrain by half (rounded down) for your group, but never ignore them completely.

- **Landmark Recall:** You have advantage on Intelligence (History) or Intelligence (Nature) checks to recall the location of natural landmarks, settlements, or other notable geographic features you’ve visited or studied.
