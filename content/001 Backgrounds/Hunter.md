**Tool Proficiencies:** Leatherworker's tools
**Languages:** One of your choice  
**Equipment:** Leatherworker's tools, a hunting trap, a well-maintained longbow or light crossbow with 20 ammunition, a set of traveler’s clothes, a trophy from a past hunt, and a belt pouch containing 5 gp
# Origin Feat: [[Skulker]]

You are adept at moving unseen and unheard, even while pursuing dangerous prey.

- You can try to hide when you are lightly obscured from the creature from which you are hiding.

- When you are hidden from a creature and miss it with a ranged weapon attack, making the attack doesn’t reveal your position.

- You have advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing or sight when actively tracking a creature.

- Dim light doesn’t impose disadvantage on your Wisdom (Perception) checks.
