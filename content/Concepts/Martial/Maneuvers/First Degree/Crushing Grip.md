**Prerequisites:** Strength of 11, Athletics proficiency

When you Grapple a target, you can expend one Maneuver Die to enhance your grip. The target takes bludgeoning damage equal to your Maneuver Die + your Strength modifier when you initiate the Grapple and if it attempts to escape the Grapple.
