**Prerequisites:** Proficiency in [[Acrobatics]]

On your turn, you may expend a Maneuver Die to perform any one of the following benefits.

* Jump over a creature no more than one size greater than you, landing on the opposite side.

* Gain a climb speed equal to your walking speed

* Attempt to climb onto a creature at least two sizes larger than yourself

* Make a Dexterity saving throw or ability check against an ongoing effect or spell affecting you that would normally take an action or only occur at the end of your turn

* Ignore fall damage you would have otherwise taken

* Gain the ability to walk along vertical surfaces until the end of your turn
