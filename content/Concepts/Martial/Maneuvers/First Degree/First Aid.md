**Prerequisites:** Medicine proficiency

As an action, you touch a willing, living creature and expend Maneuver Dice (up to your Proficiency Bonus).

For every Maneuver Die you spent, that creature regains hit points equal to one roll of its Hit Die + its Constitution modifier (minimum of 0).
