**Prerequisites:** Intelligence 11 or Wisdom 11

When you make an ability check using alchemist's supplies, or a herbalism or poisoner's kit, you can expend one Maneuver Die and add it to your roll.

You can do so after you roll, but before you know if you succeed or fail.
