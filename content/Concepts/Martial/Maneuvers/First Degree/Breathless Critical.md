**Prerequisites:** Constitution of 13

When you score a critical hit with a weapon attack, you can expend a Maneuver Die to knock the breath out of your target. For 1 minute, the target can't speak and it loses its reaction.

A suffocating target can make a Constitution saving throw at the start of each of its turns, ending this effect on a success.
