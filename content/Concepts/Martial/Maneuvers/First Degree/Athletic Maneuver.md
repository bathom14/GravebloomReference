**Prerequisites:** Proficiency in [[Athletics]]

On your turn, you may expend a Maneuver Die to perform any one of the following benefits.

* Jump a distance equal to your movement speed.

* Lift twice your normal lifting capacity until the end of your turn.

* Move at normal speed while carrying or dragging another creature until the end of your turn.

* Make a free Strength saving throw or ability check against an ongoing effect or spell effecting you that would normally take an action or only occur at the end of your turn

* Take the dash action as a bonus action.
