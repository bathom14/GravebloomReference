**Prerequisites:** Constitution of 11

You prepare yourself to take a hit. As a bonus action, you can expend one Maneuver Die to gain temporary hit points equal to your Maneuver Die + your Constitution modifier. Temporary hit points from this Maneuver only last for 1 minute.
