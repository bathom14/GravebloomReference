**Prerequisites:** Strength of 11

If you move at least 20 feet toward a target and hit it with a melee weapon attack, you can expend a Maneuver Die to force it to make a Strength saving throw. On a failed save, add your Maneuver Die to the damage roll and it is knocked prone.
