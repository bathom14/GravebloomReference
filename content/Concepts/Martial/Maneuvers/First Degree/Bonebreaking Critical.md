**Prerequisites:** Strength of 11

When you score a critical hit with a weapon attack, you can expend a Maneuver Die to cripple the target. For 1 minute, it deals half damage with Strength-based attacks.

A crippled target can make a Constitution saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending this effect on a success.
