**Prerequisites:** Dexterity 17

As a bonus action, you can expend a Maneuver Die to enter a heightened state of focus which requires concentration.

For the next minute, you can use your bonus action, including the bonus action used to activate this Maneuver, to make two ranged weapon attacks so long as you have ammunition.

This Maneuver's effects don't stack with the swift quiver spell.
