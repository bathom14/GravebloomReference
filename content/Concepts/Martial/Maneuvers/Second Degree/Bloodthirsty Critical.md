**Prerequisites:** Strength of 13

When you score a critical hit with a weapon attack, you can expend one Maneuver Die to instantly make one additional weapon attack against the same target. On hit, you add your Maneuver Die to the damage roll.

However, if you score a critical hit with this bonus attack, you can't use this Maneuver on that attack as well.
