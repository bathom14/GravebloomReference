**Prerequisites:** Strength of 13

When you score a critical hit with a weapon attack, you can expend a Maneuver Die to disable your target. For 1 minute, its speed is reduced to 0.

A crippled target can make a Constitution saving throw at the start of each of its turns. On a success, this effect ends.
