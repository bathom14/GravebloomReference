**Prerequisites:** Strength of 15

When you score a critical hit on a creature with a weapon attack, you can expend a Maneuver Die to strike its head. For 1 minute, it must subtract a d6 from every attack roll, ability check, or saving throw it makes to maintain concentration.

The creature makes an Intelligence saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending this effect on a success.
