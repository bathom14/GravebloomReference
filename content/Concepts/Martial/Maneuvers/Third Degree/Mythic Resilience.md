**Prerequisites:** Constitution of 15

When you take damage from a source you can see, you can expend Maneuver Dice (up to your Proficiency Bonus) to reduce the damage by three Maneuver Dice per Maneuver Die spent.

If the total rolled exceeds the amount of damage, you gain temporary hit points equal to the remaining amount.
