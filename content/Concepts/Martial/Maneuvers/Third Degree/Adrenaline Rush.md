**Prerequisites:** Constitution of 15

As a bonus action, you can expend a Maneuver Die to flood your body with adrenaline. You gain temporary hit points equal to twice your Maneuver Die + your Constitution modifier, and you have advantage on Strength checks and saving throws until the start of your next turn.
