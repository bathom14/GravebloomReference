**Prerequisites:** Deception proficiency

You can expend a Maneuver Die to spend 1 minute giving a cunning speech to a number of creatures that can hear you equal to your level.

At the conclusion, creatures who heard the entire speech must make a Wisdom saving throw.

All creatures who fail this saving throw become hostile toward a creature or location of your choice for 10 minutes.
