**Prerequisites:** Strength of 15

When you hit a creature with a melee weapon attack, you can expend a Maneuver Die to disorient the target. It must make a Constitution saving throw or it has disadvantage on attack rolls and ability checks, and it can't take reactions until the end of its next turn.
