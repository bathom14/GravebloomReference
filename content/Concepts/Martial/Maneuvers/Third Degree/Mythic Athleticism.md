**Prerequisites:** Strength or Dexterity of 15

As a bonus action, you can expend a Maneuver Die to supercharge your physique. For 1 minute, your jump distance is tripled, you have advantage on Strength (Athletics) and Dexterity (Acrobatics) checks, and your speed increases by 10 feet.
