# Travel Roles



### Navigator
***Skill.*** Survival (Wisdom)
***Minor Skill.*** Nature (Intelligence)
***Tool Proficiency.*** Navigator's Tools
***Tool Requirement.*** Map (Optional)

**While Traversing.**
Attempts the Navigation check upon moving to a new hex.

**While Stationary.**

### Trailblazer
***Skill.*** Survival (Wisdom)
***Minor Skill.*** Nature (Intelligence)
***Tool Proficiency.*** Woodcarver's Tools
***Tools.*** Slashing Melee Weapon (Required)

Travel Pace Modifier: 0.5x

**While Traversing.**

Will apply a Travel Pace Modifier of x1/2

Will add one level of Trail completion of Traversal through a Hex.

**While Stationary.**

Will add one level of Trail to Hex.

### Sentinel
***Major Skill.*** Perception (Wisdom)
***Minor Skill.*** Survival (Wisdom)
***Tool Proficiency.*** None
***Tools.*** None

**While Traversing.** 

May allow for reducing Surprise Encounters to Normal Encounters.

### Scout
***Major Skill.*** Stealth (Dexterity)
***Minor Skill.*** Survival (Wisdom)
***Tool Proficiency.*** None
***Tools.*** None

**While Traversing.**
If Movement Speed > Expedition Movement Speed, may proceed party, potentially allowing for the avoidance of Surprise Encounters or granting a bonus to Initiative for Normal Encounters.

**While Stationary.**
May forward scout the path to the next hex, potentially allowing for the avoidance of Surprise Encounters or granting a bonus to Initiative for Normal Encounters.
