*Necromancy cantrip*

You spray toxic mist at a creature within range. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes {@damage 1d12} Poison damage.

**At Higher Levels.** **Cantrip Upgrade.** The damage increases by {@damage 1d12} when you reach levels 5 ({@damage 2d12}), 11 ({@damage 3d12}), and 17 ({@damage 4d12}).
