*Evocation spell level 5*

You create a Large hand of shimmering magical energy in an unoccupied space that you can see within range. The hand lasts for the duration, and it moves at your command, mimicking the movements of your own hand.

**At Higher Levels.** **Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** The damage of the Clenched Fist increases by {@scaledamage 4d8|5-9|2d8} and the damage of the Grasping Hand increases by {@scaledamage 2d6|5-9|2d6} for each spell slot level above 5.
