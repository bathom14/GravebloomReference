*Abjuration spell level 1*

A creature of your choice that you can see within range regains {@variantrule Hit Points|XPHB} equal to {@dice 2d4} plus your spellcasting ability modifier.

**At Higher Levels.** **Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** The healing increases by {@scaledice 2d4|1-9|2d4} for each spell slot level above 1.
