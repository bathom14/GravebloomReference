*Abjuration spell level 1*

A creature you touch regains a number of {@variantrule Hit Points|XPHB} equal to {@dice 2d8} plus your spellcasting ability modifier.

**At Higher Levels.** **Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** The healing increases by {@scaledice 2d8|1-9|2d8} for each spell slot level above 1.
