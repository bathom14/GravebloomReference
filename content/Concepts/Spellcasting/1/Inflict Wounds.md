*Necromancy spell level 1*

A creature you touch makes a Constitution saving throw, taking {@damage 2d10} Necrotic damage on a failed save or half as much damage on a successful one.

**At Higher Levels.** **Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** The damage increases by {@scaledamage 2d10|1-9|1d10} for each spell slot level above 1.
