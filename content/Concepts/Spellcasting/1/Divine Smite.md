*Evocation spell level 1*

The target takes an extra {@damage 2d8} Radiant damage from the attack. The damage increases by {@damage 1d8} if the target is a Fiend or an Undead.

**At Higher Levels.** **Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** The damage increases by {@scaledamage 2d8|1-9|1d8} for each spell slot level above 1.
