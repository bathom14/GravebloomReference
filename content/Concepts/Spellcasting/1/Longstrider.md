*Transmutation spell level 1*

You touch a creature. The target's {@variantrule Speed|XPHB} increases by 10 feet until the spell ends.

**At Higher Levels.** **Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** You can target one additional creature for each spell slot level above 1.
