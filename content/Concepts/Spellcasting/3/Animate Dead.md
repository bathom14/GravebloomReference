*Necromancy spell level 3*

Choose a pile of bones or a corpse of a Medium or Small Humanoid within range. The target becomes an Undead creature: a {@creature Skeleton|XMM} if you chose bones or a {@creature Zombie|XMM} if you chose a corpse.

**At Higher Levels.** **Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** You animate or reassert control over two additional Undead creatures for each spell slot level above 3. Each of the creatures must come from a different corpse or pile of bones.
