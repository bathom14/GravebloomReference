*Necromancy spell level 3*

You call forth spirits of the dead, which flit around you for the spell's duration. The spirits are intangible and invulnerable.

**At Higher Levels.** **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the damage increases by {@scaledamage 1d8|3,5,7,9|1d8} for every two slot levels above 3rd.
