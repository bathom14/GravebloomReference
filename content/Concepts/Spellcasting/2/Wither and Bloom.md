*Necromancy spell level 2*

You invoke both death and life upon a 10-foot-radius sphere centered on a point within range. Each creature of your choice in that area must make a Constitution saving throw, taking {@damage 2d6} necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. Nonmagical vegetation in that area withers.

**At Higher Levels.** **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by {@scaledamage 2d6|2-9|1d6} for each slot above the 2nd, and the number of Hit Dice that can be spent and added to the healing roll increases by one for each slot above 2nd.
