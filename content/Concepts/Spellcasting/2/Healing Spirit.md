*Conjuration spell level 2*

You call forth a nature spirit to soothe the wounded. The intangible spirit appears in a space that is a 5-foot cube you can see within range. The spirit looks like a transparent beast or fey (your choice).

**At Higher Levels.** **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the healing increases by {@scaledice 1d6|2-9|1d6} for each slot level above 2nd.
