*Transmutation spell level 2*

You touch a creature and choose Strength, Dexterity, Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma. For the duration, the target has {@variantrule Advantage|XPHB} on ability checks using the chosen ability.

**At Higher Levels.** **Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** You can target one additional creature for each spell slot level above 2. You can choose a different ability for each target.
