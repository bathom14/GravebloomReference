*Conjuration spell level 2*

Choose an unoccupied 5-foot cube of air that you can see within range. An elemental force that resembles a dust devil appears in the cube and lasts for the spell's duration.

**At Higher Levels.** **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by {@scaledamage 1d8|2-9|1d8} for each slot level above 2nd.
