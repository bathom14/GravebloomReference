# Psionic Power

Various features may awaken latent psionic power within your character. Psionic power, unlike spellcasting, is an internally derived source of supernatural capabilities. If managed properly, you may used psionic powers perpetually, but managed improperly you may temporarily exhaust your reserves of power. Additionally, the powers and effects of your psionic abilities scale relative to your overall psionic power reserves.

Once given a feature grants you a Psionic Power, you are given a permanent Psionic Power Die.
# Psionic Power Die

Representing your psionic power levels is the **Psionic Power Die** (dΨ). Your Psionic Power Die has an initial size, corresponding to your level. At level 1, the initial size of the dΨ is a d6. It increases to a d8 at level 5, a d10 at level 11, and a d12 at level 17. 

Psionic abilities may dictate that you make a roll of your dΨ. If an ability dictates that you roll your dΨ and you roll the highest value, your dΨ *shrinks* by one size. This represents you burning through your psionic reserves. For example, if the die is a d6 and your roll a 6, it becomes a d4. If it's a d4 and you roll a 4, it becomes unusable until you finish a long rest or an ability causes your dΨ to *grow*. In this state, your dΨ is *depleted*

Conversely, if you roll a 1 on your Psionic Talent die, it *grows* by one die size after the roll, up to its starting size. This represents a conservation of psionic energy for later use. For example, if you roll a 1 on a d4, the die then becomes d6. You may not grow your dΨ beyond its initial size, unless a feature states otherwise.

In addition to rolls having the potential to shrink or grow your dΨ, certain abilities may dictate that you automatically shrink or grow the size of your dΨ. For example, if an ability dictated that you decrease the size of your dΨ while it is a d4, it becomes unusable. Conversely, if your dΨ is currently exhausted and an ability dictates that you increase the size of your dΨ, your dΨ is returned as a d4.

Whenever you finish a long rest, your dΨ resets to its initial size.

**Psi Replenishment.** As a bonus action, you can calm your mind for a moment and restore your dΨ to its starting size. You then can't use Psi Replenishment until your finish a long rest.

# Psionic Values

Certain psionic powers may depend on derived values to determine their effects. 

Your **Psionic Index** (λ dΨ) is the current stratum of your psionic power, as represented by your dΨ size. When your dΨ is exhausted (i.e. d0), your λ dΨ = 0. Each die size increments your λ dΨ by one, starting with λ dΨ = 1 at a d4, and increasing up to λ dΨ = 6 at a d20.

Your **Psionic Bandwidth** (dΨₘₐₓ) is the maximum possible roll for your current dΨ. For example, if your current dΨ is a d6, your dΨₘₐₓ is 6.

Your Psionic Reserve (ΣdΨ) is the maximum possible roll for your current dΨ, as well as the maximum possible roll for each lower size of dΨ you have remaining to shrink into. For example, if your dΨ is a d6, your ΣdΨ = 6 + 4 = 10. If your dΨ is a d12, your ΣdΨ = 12 + 10 + 8 + 6 + 4 = 40.

All derived Psionic Values can be found on the Psionic reference chart on the top of this page.
