**Prerequisites:** None

When you hit a creature with a melee weapon attack, you can expend a Maneuver Die to attempt to hit any number of creatures within your reach. Make one attack roll and compare it against the AC of each chosen creature. On a hit, the target takes your normal weapon damage plus your Maneuver Die.
