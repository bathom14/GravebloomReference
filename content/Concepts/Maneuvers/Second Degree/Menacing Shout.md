**Prerequisites:** Constitution or Charisma of 13

As a bonus action, you can expend a Maneuver Die to force a creature within 30 feet that can hear you to make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failure, it is frightened of you until the end of your next turn, or until you are incapacitated.
