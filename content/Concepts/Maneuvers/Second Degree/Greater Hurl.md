**Prerequisites:** Strength of 13, Athletics proficiency

In place of an attack, you can expend a Maneuver Die to touch a target at least one size smaller than you, forcing it to make a Strength saving throw. On a failure, you throw it to a space you can see within 30 feet. The target can choose to fail.

An unwilling target falls prone if it lands in an unoccupied space. If it hits another target, that target must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or it takes bludgeoning damage equal to your Maneuver Die + your Strength modifier.

A feature that increases the size of targets you can grapple increases the size of targets you can throw with this Maneuver.
