**Prerequisites:** Strength of 13

When you hit a creature with a melee weapon attack, you can expend a Maneuver Die to rend the target. It takes extra slashing damage equal to your Maneuver Die and must make a Constitution saving throw or suffer ongoing bleed damage equal to your proficiency bonus at the start of each of its turns for 1 minute. The creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the bleed on a success.
