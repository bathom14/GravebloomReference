**Prerequisites:** Strength of 13

When you hit a creature with a melee weapon attack, you can expend a Maneuver Die to strike with force. It must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or until the start of your next turn its speed is 0, it can't speak, it has disadvantage on Dexterity saving throws, and attack rolls against it have advantage.
