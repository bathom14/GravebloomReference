**Prerequisites:** Constitution or Charisma of 15

As an action, you can expend a Maneuver Die to issue forth a mighty cry, forcing creatures of your choice that can hear you in an adjacent 30‑foot cone to make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, creatures drop whatever they are holding and are frightened of you for 1 minute.

If a frightened creature can't see or hear you, it can use its action to repeat the saving throw, ending the effect on a success.
