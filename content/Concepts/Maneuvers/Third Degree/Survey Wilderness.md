**Prerequisites:** Wisdom of 15

As an action, you can expend a Maneuver Die to immediately determine one of the following about the surrounding wilderness within 1 mile (or within 6 miles in favored terrain, if you have it): the location of fresh water, the location of natural shelter, or the presence and general direction of a significant beast or monstrosity.
