**Prerequisites:** [[Fighter]], [[Barbarian]]

When you hit a creature with a melee weapon attack, you can expend one superiority die to attempt to perform a brutal whirlwind slash. You may add your maneuver die to your original attack roll. If this new value would hit any creature within your reach with the same melee weapon, that creature is hit with the whirlwind slash and recieves the normal damage roll of your weapon plus the value of your maneuver dice roll.
