**Prerequisites:** —

You gain proficiency with either Animal Handling or vehicles (land). If you are already proficient, you instead gain expertise in the chosen skill.

While mounted, you and your mount can both move on the same turn when you take the Dash action (your mount still uses its action to Dash).
