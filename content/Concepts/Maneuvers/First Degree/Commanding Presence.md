**Prerequisites:** Charisma 11 or Strength 11

Whenever you make an Intimidation or Persuasion check, you can expend a Maneuver Die and add it to your roll.

You can do so after you roll, but before you know if you succeed or fail.

You can also make Strength (Intimidation) checks in place of the normal Charisma (Intimidation) checks.
