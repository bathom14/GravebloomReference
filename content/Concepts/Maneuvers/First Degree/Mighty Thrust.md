**Prerequisites:** Strength of 11, Athletics proficiency

In place of an attack, you expend a Maneuver Die to force one target that you touch to make a Strength saving throw. On a failure, it is knocked back in a line a number of feet equal to 5 times your Strength modifier (minimum 5 feet). This distance is halved for every size category the target is larger than you.
