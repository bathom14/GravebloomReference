**Prerequisites:** Strength of 11, Athletics proficiency

In place of an attack, you expend a Maneuver Die to throw one object you are holding at a target you can see within 60 feet. The target must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or both the thrown object and target take bludgeoning damage equal to twice your Maneuver Die + your Strength modifier.

At 11th level, the range of this Maneuver becomes 120 feet.
