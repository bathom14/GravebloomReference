---
title: "Roll"
note_type: "[[Maneuver]]"
degree: "[[First Degree]]"
prerequisites: "None"
---

**Prerequisites:** None

On your turn, when a creature rolls an attack roll targeted at you, you may expend a maneuver die to roll away. You may add your expended die to your AC for the attack. After it completes, you must move to a location 5 ft away from your initial position.
