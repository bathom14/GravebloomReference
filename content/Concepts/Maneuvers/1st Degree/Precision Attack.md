**Prerequisites:** [[Fighter]], [[Monk]], [[Ranger]], [[Rogue]]

As part of a weapon attack you can expend one Maneuver Die and add it to your attack roll. You can use this Maneuver after you roll, but before you know if your attack is a hit or miss.
