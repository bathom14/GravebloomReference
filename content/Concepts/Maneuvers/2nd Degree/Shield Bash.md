**Prerequisites:** [[Barbarian]], [[Fighter]], [[Monk]], [[Paladin]]

When you land a melee attack on an enemy while wielding a shield, you may expend a maneuver die to perform a shield bash. Roll your maneuver die, and add the result to your damage on the attack.
