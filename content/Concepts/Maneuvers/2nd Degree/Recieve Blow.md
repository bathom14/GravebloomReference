**Prerequisites:** [[Barbarian]], [[Fighter]], [[Paladin]]

Whenever a creature standing immediately adjacent to you recieves damage from an enemy, you may expend a maneuver die to instead take the damage yourself. You may reduce the damage taken by an amount equal to a roll of your maneuver die
