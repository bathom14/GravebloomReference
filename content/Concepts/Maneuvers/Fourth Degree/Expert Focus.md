**Prerequisites:** None

As an action, you expend one Maneuver Die to temporarily focus on one of your skills. Choose a skill or tool you are proficient in.

For 1 hour, whenever you make an ability check with that skill you gain a bonus to your roll equal to your Maneuver Die.
