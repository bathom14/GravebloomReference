**Prerequisites:** Constitution of 17

When you take damage that would reduce you to 0 hit points, even if that damage would normally kill you outright, you can expend Maneuver Dice (up to your Proficiency Bonus) and fall to 1 hit point instead. You gain temporary hit points equal to two Maneuver Dice per Maneuver Die spent.
