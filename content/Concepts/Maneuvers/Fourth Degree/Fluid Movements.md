**Prerequisites:** Dexterity 17

As a bonus action, you can expend one Maneuver Die to enter a heightened state of focus, which requires your concentration.

For the next minute, your speed can't be reduced, opportunity attacks against you have disadvantage, and you are immune to the Grappled, Paralyzed, and Restrained conditions.
