**Prerequisites:** Strength of 17

When you hit a creature with a melee weapon attack, you can expend a Maneuver Die to attempt to banish it. The target must succeed on a Charisma saving throw or be banished as if by the banishment spell (no concentration required) until the end of your next turn.
