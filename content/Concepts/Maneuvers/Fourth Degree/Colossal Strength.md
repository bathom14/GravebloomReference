**Prerequisites:** Strength of 17

As a bonus action, you can expend Maneuver Dice (up to your proficiency bonus) to enhance your strength to mythic levels, though this Maneuver's effect requires your concentration.

For 10 minutes, the amount of weight you can push, drag, pull, or lift is multiplied by the amount in the table below:

Maneuver Dice Total | Weight
--- | ---
Normal | 30 × Strength score
1 | 50 × Strength score
2 | 100 × Strength score
3 | 150 × Strength score
4 | 200 × Strength score
5 | 250 × Strength score
6 | 300 × Strength score

For each size category you are above Medium, the amount of weight you can move as part of this Maneuver is doubled.
