# Maneuvers

---
All martial classes gain access to a number of Maneuver Die and access to Maneuvers. 

For Full Martials (Barbarian, Fighter, Monk, Rogue), this number is equal to their proficiency bonus. 

For Half Martials (Paladin, Ranger, Artificer), this number is equal to one-half of their proficiency bonus, rounded up. 

For Third Martials (College of Swords, Bladesinger, Hexblade, etc..), this number is equal to one-third of their proficiency bonus. 

The size of these superiority die is determined by class and level, and often changes as levels progress. The chart below details the general trend of maneuver progression. This is a guideline, not a rule, and specific beats general.

You can prepare a number of maneuvers each long rest from among the list of maneuvers available to you. Each time you complete a long rest, you may prepare a different selection of maneuvers.

Your list of available maneuvers is all maneuvers on this universal list which you qualify for, dependent on class, subclass, and maneuver die size, as well as any maneuvers granted to you through other means. As your maneuver die increases in size as you progress through levels in a martial class, you will gain access to higher Degrees of maneuvers. 

All maneuvers require you to expend a maneuver die to perform them. If they indicate that the maneuver die is used to modify another roll or property, this indicates that the maneuver die will be rolled and its result used.

Some classes and subclasses may have additional maneuvers available to them, indicated in their class or subclass documentation.

### First Degree
#### Any Maneuver Die Size

**Acrobatic Maneuver**
Prerequisite: Proficiency in Acrobatics
On your turn, you may expend a Maneuver Die to perform any one of the following benefits.
* Jump over a creature no more than one size greater than you, landing on the opposite side.
* Gain a climb speed equal to your walking speed
* Attempt to climb onto a creature at least two sizes larger than yourself
* Make a Dexterity saving throw or ability check against an ongoing effect or spell affecting you that would normally take an action or only occur at the end of your turn
* Ignore fall damage you would have otherwise taken
* Gain the ability to walk along vertical surfaces until the end of your turn

**Athletic Maneuver**
Prerequisite: Proficiency in Athletics
On your turn, you may expend a Maneuver Die to perform any one of the following benefits.
* Jump a distance equal to your movement speed.
* Lift twice your normal lifting capacity until the end of your turn.
* Move at normal speed while carrying or dragging another creature until the end of your turn.
* Make a free Strength saving throw or ability check against an ongoing effect or spell effecting you that would normally take an action or only occur at the end of your turn
* Take the dash action as a bonus action.

**Quick Retreat** 
Prerequisite: None 
When a creature moves within 10 feet of you, you may expend a maneuver die and use your reaction to immediately take the Disengage action and move a distance up to half of your speed.

**Roll** 
Prerequisite: None 
On your turn, when a creature rolls an attack roll targeted at you, you may expend a maneuver die to roll away. You may add your expended die to your AC for the attack. After it completes, you must move to a location 5 ft away from your initial position.



### Second Degree
#### Maneuver Die Size D6 or Greater

**Ambush** 
Prerequisite: Fighter, Monk, Ranger, Rogue 
When you make a Dexterity (Stealth) check or an initiative roll, you can expend one superiority die and add the die to the roll, provided you aren't incapacitated.

**Bait and Switch** 
Prerequisite: Fighter, Monk, Ranger, Rogue 
When you're within 5 feet of a creature on your turn, you can expend one superiority die and switch places with that creature, provided you spend at least 5 feet of movement and the creature is willing and isn't incapacitated. This movement doesn't provoke opportunity attacks.
:
Roll the superiority die. Until the start of your next turn, you or the other creature (your choice) gains a bonus to AC equal to the number rolled.

**Brace** 
Prerequisite: Barbarian, Fighter, Monk, Paladin, Rogue 
When a creature you can see moves into the reach you have with the melee weapon you're wielding, you can use your reaction to expend one superiority die and make one attack against the creature, using that weapon. If the attack hits, add the superiority die to the weapon's damage roll.

**Backstab** 
Prerequisite: Rogue 
When you attack a creature from their back with a melee weapon, you may expend a maneuver die to make the attack with advantage. If it hits, you may add the expended maneuver die to the damage.

**Commander's Strike** 
Prerequisite: Fighter, Paladin 
When you take the Attack action on your
turn, you can forgo one of your attacks and use a bonus action to direct one of your companions to strike. When you do so, choose a friendly creature who can see or hear you and expend one superiority die. That creature can immediately use its reaction to make one weapon attack, adding the superiority die to the attack's damage roll.

**Commanding Presence** 
Prerequisite: Barbarian, Fighter, Paladin 
When you make a Charisma (Intimidation), a Charisma (Performance), or a Charisma (Persuasion) check, you can expend one superiority die and add the superiority die to the ability check.

**Disarming Attack** 
Prerequisite: Barbarian, Fighter, Monk, Ranger, Rogue 
When you hit a creature with a weapon attack, you can expend one superiority die to attempt to disarm the target, forcing it to drop one item of your choice that it's holding. You add the superiority die to the attack's damage roll, and the target must make a Strength saving throw. On a failed save, it drops the object you choose. The object lands at its feet.

**Distracting Strike** 
Prerequisite: Fighter, Monk, Ranger, Rogue 
When you hit a creature with a weapon attack, you can expend one superiority die to distract the creature, giving your allies an opening. You add the superiority die to the attack's damage roll. The next attack roll against the target by an attacker other than you has advantage if the attack is made before the start of your next turn.

**Evasive Footwork** 
Prerequisite: Fighter, Monk, Ranger, Rogue 
When you move, you can expend one superiority die, rolling the die and adding the number rolled to your AC until you stop moving.

**Feinting Attack** 
Prerequisite: Fighter, Monk, Ranger, Rogue 
You can expend one superiority die and use a bonus action on your turn to feint, choosing one creature within 5 feet of you as your target. You have advantage on your next attack roll against that creature this turn. If that attack hits, add the superiority die to the attack's damage roll.


**Goading Attack** 
Prerequisite: Barbarian, Fighter, Monk, Paladin, Ranger, Rogue 
When you hit a creature with a weapon attack, you can expend one superiority die to attempt to goad the target into attacking you. You add the superiority die to the attack's damage roll, and the target must make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, the target has disadvantage on all attack rolls against targets other than you until the end of your next turn.




**Grappling Strike** 
Prerequisite: Barbarian, Fighter, Monk, Rogue 
Immediately after you hit a creature with a melee attack on your turn, you can expend one superiority die and then try to grapple the target as a bonus action (see the Player's Handbook for rules on grappling). Add the superiority die to your Strength (Athletics) check.

**Invigorating Kill** 
Prerequisite: Barbarian 
While you are raging, you may choose to expend a manevuer die after you kill a creature to extend the duuration of your rage by 1 minute.

**Lunging Attack** 
Prerequisite: Barbarian, Fighter, Monk, Paladin, Ranger, Rogue 
When you make a melee weapon attack on your turn, you can expend one superiority die to increase your reach for that attack by 5 feet. If you hit, you add the superiority die to the attack's damage roll.


**Maneuvering Attack** 
Prerequisite: Fighter, Paladin 
When you hit a creature with a weapon attack, you can expend one superiority die to maneuver one of your comrades into a more advantageous position. You add the superiority die to the attack's damage roll, and you choose a friendly creature who can see or hear you. That creature can use its reaction to move up to half its speed without provoking opportunity attacks from the target of your attack.

**Martyr's Retribution** 
Prerequisite: Zealot (Barbarian), Paladin, War Domain (Cleric), Tempest Domain (Cleric) 

Whenever a creature immediately adjacent to you is hit with an attack, you may expend a maneuver die to recieve the damage for that attack instead. After recieving the damage, you may immediately make a free attack against the creature that made the attack and add your expended superiority die to the damage if it hits.

**Meat Shield** 
Prerequisite: Barbarian, Fighter 
When you are attacked while you are grappling a creature, you may expend a maneuver die to redirect the attack to the grappled creature. You may add your maneuver die to the damage if it hits.

**Menacing Attack** 
Prerequisite: Barbarian, Fighter, Paladin 
When you hit a creature with a weapon attack, you can expend one superiority die to attempt to frighten the target. You add the superiority die to the attack's damage roll, and the target must make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, it is frightened of you until the end of your next turn.

**Parry** 
Prerequisite: Fighter, Monk, Ranger, Rogue 
When another creature damages you with a melee attack, you can use your reaction and expend one superiority die to reduce the damage by the number you roll on your superiority die + your Dexterity modifier.

**Precision Attack** 
Prerequisite: Fighter, Monk, Ranger, Rogue 
When you make a weapon attack roll against a creature, you can expend one superiority die to add it to the roll. You can use this maneuver before or after making the attack roll, but before any effects of the attack are applied.

**Pushing Attack** 
Prerequisite: Barbarian, Fighter, Paladin 
When you hit a creature with a weapon attack, you can expend one superiority die to attempt to drive the target back. You add the superiority die to the attack's damage roll, and if the target is Large or smaller, it must make a Strength saving throw. On a failed save, you push the target up to 15 feet away from you.

**Quick Toss** 
Prerequisite: Barbarian, Fighter, Monk, Rogue 
As a bonus action, you can expend one superiority die and make a ranged attack with a weapon that has the thrown property. You can draw the weapon as part of making this attack. If you hit, add the superiority die to the weapon's damage roll.

**Rally** 
Prerequisite: Fighter, Paladin 
On your turn, you can use a bonus action and expend one superiority die to bolster the resolve of one of your companions. When you do so, choose a friendly creature who can see or hear you. That creature gains temporary hit points equal to the superiority die roll + your Charisma modifier.

**Recieve Blow** 
Prerequisite: Barbarian, Fighter, Paladin 
Whenever a creature standing immediately adjacent to you recieves damage from an enemy, you may expend a maneuver die to instead take the damage yourself. You may reduce the damage taken by an amount equal to a roll of your maneuver die

**Returning Strike** 
Prerequisite: Barbarian, Fighter, Monk 
When another creature damages you with a melee attack, you can use your reaction and expend one superiority die to make free melee attack on that creature. If you hit, you may add the roll of your superiority die to the damage.

**Riposte** 
Prerequisite: Fighter, Monk, Rogue 
When a creature misses you with a melee attack, you can use your reaction and expend one superiority die to make a melee weapon attack against the creature. If you hit, you add the superiority die to the attack's damage roll.


**Serenity** 
Prerequisite: Monk 
On your turn, you can use a bonus action and expend one superiority die to briefly steady your mind, restoring a number of Ki points equal to your proficiency bonus.

**Shield Bash** 
Prerequisite: Barbarian, Fighter, Monk, Paladin 
When you land a melee attack on an enemy while wielding a shield, you may expend a maneuver die to perform a shield bash. Roll your maneuver die, and add the result to your damage on the attack.

**Sweeping Attack** 
Prerequisite: Barbarian, Fighter, Paladin 
When you hit a creature with a melee weapon attack, you can expend one superiority die to attempt to damage another creature with the same attack. Choose another creature within 5 feet of the original target and within your reach. If the original attack roll would hit the second creature, it takes damage equal to the number you roll on your superiority die. The damage is of the same type dealt by the original attack.

**Tactical Assessment** 
Prerequisite: Artificer, Fighter, Monk, Ranger, Rogue 
When you make an Intelligence (Investigation), an Intelligence (History), or a Wisdom (Insight) check, you can expend one superiority die and add the superiority die to the ability check.

**Tactician's Eye** 
Prerequisite: Fighter (Strategist)
When you employ a Strategem, you may expend a maneuver die to size up your enemies. You may select a number of creatures up to your Strategic Capacity, and the DM will tell you whether they are above or below half of their hit points. 

**Trip Attack** 
Prerequisite: Barbarian, Fighter, Monk, Rogue 
When you hit a creature with a weapon attack, you can expend one superiority die to attempt to knock the target down. You add the superiority die to the attack's damage roll, and if the target is Large or smaller, it must make a Strength saving throw. On a failed save, you knock the target prone.


### Thrid Degree
#### Maneuver Die Size d8 or Greater

**Identify Weakness** 
Prerequisite: Strategist (Fighter) 
When you attack a creature, you may expend a maneuver die to attempt to identify their weaknesses. You may add the expended maneuver die to your attack roll. If it hits, the DM will inform you of a number of damage resistances, immunities, and vulnerabilities of the creature, up to your Strategic Capacity.

**Spin Attack** 
Prerequisite: Barbarian, Fighter 
When you hit a creature with a melee weapon attack, you can expend one superiority die to attempt to damage all other creatures surrounding you with the same attack. If the original attack roll would hit any creature within your reach with the melee weapon you used for the initial attack, it takes damage equal to the number you roll on your superiority die. The damage is of the same type dealt by the original attack.



### Fourth Degree
#### Maneuver Die Size d10 or Greater
**Whirlwind Slash** 
Prerequisite: Fighter, Barbarian 
When you hit a creature with a melee weapon attack, you can expend one superiority die to attempt to perform a brutal whirlwind slash. You may add your maneuver die to your original attack roll. If this new value would hit any creature within your reach with the same melee weapon, that creature is hit with the whirlwind slash and recieves the normal damage roll of your weapon plus the value of your maneuver dice roll.

**Nested Strategy** 
Prerequisite: Strategist (Fighter) 
When you employ a Strategem, you may expend a maneuver die to employ an additional Strategem as a free action. The additional stratagem must be one you have prepared, but this usage does expend the Stratagem.


### Fifth Degree
#### Maneuver Die Size d12 or Greater

**The Nine Situations** 
Prerequisite: Strategist (Fighter) 
When you or a creature you can see attacks a creature, you may expend a superiority die to give a number of creatures you can see up to your Strategic Capacity advantage until your next turn. In addition, all attacks made by the chosen creatures may add your Strategic Capacity to their attack and damage rolls.
