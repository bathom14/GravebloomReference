# Fragment of Abhorrence
*Fragment of Suffering*  

## Benefit

Once on each of your turns when you hit a creature with a weapon attack roll, you can force the target to move up to 10 feet away from you in a direction of your choice. A creature that can't be [frightened](rules/5e/conditions.md#Frightened) is immune to this effect.

## Drawback

If you start your turn [frightened](rules/5e/conditions.md#Frightened), you take `dice:2d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`2d6`) psychic damage.

*Source: Critical Role: Call of the Netherdeep p. 214*
