# Keranos's Devotee
*Piety Trait*  

*Piety 3+ Keranos trait*

As a devotee of Keranos, you have proven your wisdom and your allegiance to the storm lord. Once on each of your turns when you hit a creature with a weapon attack, you can deal an extra `dice:1d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`d6`) lightning damage to the target. You can use this trait a number of times equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum of once). You regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest.

*Source: Mythic Odysseys of Theros p. 55*
