# Charm of Invisibility

This charm allows you to cast the [invisibility](compendium/5e/spells/invisibility.md) spell as an action, no components required. Once used three times, this charm vanishes from you.

*Source: Icewind Dale: Rime of the Frostmaiden p. 228*
