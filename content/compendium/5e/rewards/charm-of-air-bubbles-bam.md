# Charm of Air Bubbles

> [!note]
> This charm can be bestowed by a [chwinga astronaut](compendium/5e/bestiary/elemental/chwinga-astronaut-bam.md) using their Magical Gift action.

This charm allows you to cast the [Air Bubble](compendium/5e/spells/air-bubble-aag.md) spell (see the Astral Adventurer's Guide) as an action. Once used three times, this charm goes away.

*Source: Boo's Astral Menagerie p. 17*
