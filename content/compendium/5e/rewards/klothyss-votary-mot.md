# Klothys's Votary
*Piety Trait*  

*Piety 10+ Klothys trait*

You can cast [clairvoyance](compendium/5e/spells/clairvoyance.md) with this trait, requiring no material components. Once you cast the spell in this way, you can't do so again until you finish a long rest. Wisdom is your spellcasting ability for this spell.

In addition, you have advantage on saving throws against being [charmed](rules/5e/conditions.md#Charmed).

*Source: Mythic Odysseys of Theros p. 58*
