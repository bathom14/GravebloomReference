# Charm of Many Tongues

You can speak and understand all languages, you gain proficiency in the [Deception](rules/5e/skills.md#Deception) and [Persuasion](rules/5e/skills.md#Persuasion) skills, and you can add double your normal proficiency bonus when using those skills. This charm vanishes from you when you die.

*Source: Baldur's Gate: Descent Into Avernus p. 215*
