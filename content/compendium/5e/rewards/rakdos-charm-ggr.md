# Rakdos Charm
*Charm*  

When you activate this charm, you can cast the [crown of madness](compendium/5e/spells/crown-of-madness.md), [hellish rebuke](compendium/5e/spells/hellish-rebuke.md), or [shatter](compendium/5e/spells/shatter.md) spell. The charm vanishes after you activate it.

*Source: Guildmasters' Guide to Ravnica p. 174*
