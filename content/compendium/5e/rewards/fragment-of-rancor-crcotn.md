# Fragment of Rancor
*Fragment of Suffering*  

## Benefit

When you hit a creature with an attack, you can choose to deal either an extra `dice:2d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`2d6`) psychic damage to the creature or `dice:4d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`4d6`) psychic damage to each other creature within 5 feet of it. After you use this benefit, you must finish a short or long rest before you can use it again.

## Drawback

Whenever you are not [unconscious](rules/5e/conditions.md#Unconscious) and fail a saving throw, you take `dice:2d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`2d6`) psychic damage.

*Source: Critical Role: Call of the Netherdeep p. 214*
