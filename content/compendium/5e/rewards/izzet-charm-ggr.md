# Izzet Charm
*Charm*  

When you activate this charm, you can cast the [dispel magic](compendium/5e/spells/dispel-magic.md) or [lightning bolt](compendium/5e/spells/lightning-bolt.md) spell. Alternatively, as an action, you can regain one expended spell slot of 3rd level or lower. The charm vanishes after you activate it.

*Source: Guildmasters' Guide to Ravnica p. 174*
