# Charm of the Slayer

One weapon in your possession becomes a [Dragon Slayer](compendium/5e/items/dragon-slayer.md) or [Giant Slayer](compendium/5e/items/giant-slayer.md) (DM's choice) for the next 9 days. The Charm then vanishes from you, and the weapon returns to normal.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 99*
