# Ephara's Disciple
*Piety Trait*  

*Piety 25+ Ephara trait*

You can cast [Mordenkainen's private sanctum](compendium/5e/spells/mordenkainens-private-sanctum.md) with this trait, requiring no material components. Once you cast the spell in this way, you can't do so again until you finish a long rest. Intelligence is your spellcasting ability for this spell.

*Source: Mythic Odysseys of Theros p. 40*
