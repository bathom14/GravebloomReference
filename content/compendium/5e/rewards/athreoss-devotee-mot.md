# Athreos's Devotee
*Piety Trait*  

*Piety 3+ Athreos trait*

Your life is intertwined with the fate of the dead. You can cast [gentle repose](compendium/5e/spells/gentle-repose.md) with this trait, requiring no material components, a number of times equal to your Wisdom modifier (minimum of once). You regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest. Wisdom is your spellcasting ability for this spell.

*Source: Mythic Odysseys of Theros p. 37*
