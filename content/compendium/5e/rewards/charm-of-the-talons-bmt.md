# Charm of the Talons

As an action, you can cast [Dispel Magic](compendium/5e/spells/dispel-magic.md), requiring no spell components and using your Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma as the spellcasting ability (your choice). If you successfully end any spells with it, you gain `dice:1d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`d6`) "temporary hit points" for each spell level of the highest-level spell ended. Once used three times, the charm vanishes from you.

*Source: The Book of Many Things p. 62*
