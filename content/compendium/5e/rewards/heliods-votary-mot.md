# Heliod's Votary
*Piety Trait*  

*Piety 10+ Heliod trait*

You can cast [daylight](compendium/5e/spells/daylight.md) with this trait. Once you cast the spell in this way, you can't do so again until you finish a long rest. Wisdom is your spellcasting ability for this spell.

*Source: Mythic Odysseys of Theros p. 46*
