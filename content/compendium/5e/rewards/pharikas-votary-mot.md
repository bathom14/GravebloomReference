# Pharika's Votary
*Piety Trait*  

*Piety 10+ Pharika trait*

Pharika's blessing shields you from most maladies. You have advantage on saving throws against being [poisoned](rules/5e/conditions.md#Poisoned), and you are immune to disease.

*Source: Mythic Odysseys of Theros p. 70*
