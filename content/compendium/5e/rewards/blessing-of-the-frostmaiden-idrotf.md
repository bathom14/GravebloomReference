# Blessing of the Frostmaiden

Your eyes become as cold as ice. You gain immunity to cold damage. In addition, you can cast the [cone of cold](compendium/5e/spells/cone-of-cold.md) spell (save DC 15) once. You regain the ability to cast this spell when you finish a long rest.

*Source: Icewind Dale: Rime of the Frostmaiden p. 213*
