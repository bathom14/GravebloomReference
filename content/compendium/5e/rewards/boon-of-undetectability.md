# Boon of Undetectability

You gain a +10 bonus to Dexterity ([Stealth](rules/5e/skills.md#Stealth)) checks, and you can't be detected or targeted by divination magic, including scrying sensors.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 232*
