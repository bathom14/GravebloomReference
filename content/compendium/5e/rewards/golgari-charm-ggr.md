# Golgari Charm
*Charm*  

When you activate this charm, you can cast the [dispel magic](compendium/5e/spells/dispel-magic.md), [false life](compendium/5e/spells/false-life.md) (3rd-level version), or [stinking cloud](compendium/5e/spells/stinking-cloud.md) spell. The charm vanishes after you activate it.

*Source: Guildmasters' Guide to Ravnica p. 173*
