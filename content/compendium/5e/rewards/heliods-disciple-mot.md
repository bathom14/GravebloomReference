# Heliod's Disciple
*Piety Trait*  

*Piety 25+ Heliod trait*

You grow accustomed to the blazing radiance of the sun; you have advantage on saving throws against being [blinded](rules/5e/conditions.md#Blinded), and you have resistance to fire damage.

*Source: Mythic Odysseys of Theros p. 46*
