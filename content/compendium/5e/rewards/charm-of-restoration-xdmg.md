# Charm of Restoration

This Charm has 3 charges. You can expend some of its charges to cast one of the following spells: [Greater Restoration](compendium/5e/spells/greater-restoration.md) (2 charges) or [Lesser Restoration](compendium/5e/spells/lesser-restoration.md) (1 charge). Once all its charges have been expended, the Charm vanishes from you.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 99*
