# Blessing of the Lone Champion

You gain the following benefits:

- **Heroic Inspiration.** You gain [Heroic Inspiration](rules/5e/variant-rules/heroic-inspiration-xphb.md) when you finish a [Short](rules/5e/variant-rules/short-rest-xphb.md) or [Long Rest](rules/5e/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md) and whenever the DM has you roll Initiative.  
- **Temporary Hit Points.** You gain a number of [Temporary Hit Points](rules/5e/variant-rules/temporary-hit-points-xphb.md) equal to 10 times your level when you gain this blessing and when you gain a level.  

*Source: Dragon Delves*
