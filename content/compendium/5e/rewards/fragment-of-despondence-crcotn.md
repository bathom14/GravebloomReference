# Fragment of Despondence
*Fragment of Suffering*  

## Benefit

You are immune to the [charmed](rules/5e/conditions.md#Charmed) condition.

## Drawback

You can't take the [Help](rules/5e/actions.md#Help) action.

*Source: Critical Role: Call of the Netherdeep p. 214*
