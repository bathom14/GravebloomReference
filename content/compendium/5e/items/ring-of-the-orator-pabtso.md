# Ring of the Orator
*Ring, uncommon (requires attunement)*  


This ring has 6 charges. While you wear it, you can expend 1 of its charges to project your voice to be heard clearly by all creatures within 1 mile of yourself, regardless of intervening noise, for 1 minute. Magical silence, 1 foot of stone, 1 inch of common metal, a thin sheet of lead, or 3 feet of wood blocks this projection. If you project your voice while speaking a language the listening creatures don't understand, you can make the creatures understand what you're saying. You must be able to see the creatures to make them understand. The ring regains `dice:1d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`d6`) expended charges daily at dawn.

*Source: Phandelver and Below: The Shattered Obelisk p. 219*
