# Basic Poison
*Adventuring gear, poison*  

- **Properties**: [Poison](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Poison)
- **Cost**: 100 gp

As a [Bonus Action](rules/5e/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md), you can use a vial of Basic Poison to coat one weapon or up to three pieces of ammunition. A creature that takes Piercing or Slashing damage from the poisoned weapon or ammunition takes an extra `dice:1d4|noform|noparens|avg` (`d4`) Poison damage. Once applied, the poison retains potency for 1 minute or until its damage is dealt, whichever comes first.

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 227. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
