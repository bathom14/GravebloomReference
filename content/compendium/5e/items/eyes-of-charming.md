# Eyes of Charming
*Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement)*  


These crystal lenses fit over the eyes. They have 3 charges. While wearing them, you can expend 1 or more charges to cast [Charm Person](compendium/5e/spells/charm-person.md) (save DC 13). For 1 charge, you cast the level 1 version of the spell. You increase the spell's level by one for each additional charge you expend. The lenses regain all expended charges daily at dawn.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 261. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
