# Ring of Truth Telling
*Ring, uncommon (requires attunement)*  


While wearing this ring, you have advantage on Wisdom ([Insight](rules/5e/skills.md#Insight)) checks to determine whether someone is lying to you.

*Source: Waterdeep: Dragon Heist p. 192*
