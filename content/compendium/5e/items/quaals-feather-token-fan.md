# Quaal's Feather Token, Fan
*Wondrous item, uncommon*  


This object looks like a feather.

If you are on a boat or ship, you can take a [Magic](rules/5e/actions.md#Magic) action to toss the token up to 10 feet in the air. The token disappears, and a giant flapping fan takes its place. The fan floats and creates a strong wind. This wind can fill the sails of one ship, increasing its speed by 5 miles per hour for 8 hours. You can dismiss the fan as a [Magic](rules/5e/actions.md#Magic) action.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 290. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> (as "Feather Token, Fan") and the Free Rules (2024)*
