# Lash of Shadows (Dormant)
*Weapon ([whip](compendium/5e/items/whip-phb.md)), artifact (requires attunement)*  

- **Damage**: 1d4 slashing
- **Properties**: [Finesse](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Finesse), [Reach](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Reach), [Vestige of Divergence](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Vestige%20of%20Divergence)
- **Weight**: 3.0 lbs.

This snakeskin whip is touched by the power of Zehir and ends in five animated serpent heads.

## Sentience

The Lash of Shadows is a sentient chaotic evil weapon with an Intelligence of 18, a Wisdom of 16, and a Charisma of 20. It has hearing and [darkvision](rules/5e/senses.md#Darkvision) out to a range of 120 feet.

The weapon communicates telepathically with its wielder and can speak, read, and understand Abyssal, Common, and Draconic.

## Personality

A mad [marilith](compendium/5e/bestiary/fiend/marilith.md) named Sizlifeth lives within the Lash of Shadows. The weapon has a short temper and experiences only extreme emotions. It is most happy when dealing harm.

## Dormant

The whip grants the following benefits in its dormant state:

- You can speak, read, and write Abyssal and Draconic.  
- You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.  
- You can attempt to poison any creature that is hit by the whip. The creature must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or suffer the effect of one of the following poisons of your choice; once a poison other than Serpent Venom has been used, that poison can't be used again until the next dawn:  
-     - **Dead Eyes.** A creature that fails the saving throw against this poison is [poisoned](rules/5e/conditions.md#Poisoned) for 1 hour. While [poisoned](rules/5e/conditions.md#Poisoned) in this way, the creature is [blinded](rules/5e/conditions.md#Blinded).    
        - **Serpent Venom.** A creature that fails the saving throw against this poison takes `dice:3d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`3d6`) poison damage, or half as much damage on a successful save.
