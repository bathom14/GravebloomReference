# Eyescratch
*Adventuring gear, poison (contact)*  

- **Properties**: [Poison](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Poison)

A creature that comes into contact with this poison must succeed on a DC 14 Constitution saving throw or be [poisoned](rules/5e/conditions.md#Poisoned) for 1 hour and [blinded](rules/5e/conditions.md#Blinded) while [poisoned](rules/5e/conditions.md#Poisoned) in this way. A [lesser restoration](compendium/5e/spells/lesser-restoration.md) spell or similar magic ends the effect.

*Source: Waterdeep: Dragon Heist p. 211*
