# Dagger of Blindsight
*Weapon ([dagger](compendium/5e/items/dagger-phb.md)), rare (requires attunement)*  

- **Damage**: 1d4 piercing
- **Range**: 20/60
- **Properties**: [Finesse](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Finesse), [Light](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Light), [Thrown](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Thrown)
- **Weight**: 1.0 lbs.

This rare magic item requires attunement. A creature attuned to it gains [blindsight](rules/5e/senses.md#Blindsight) out to a range of 30 feet. The dagger has a saw-toothed edge and a black pearl nested in its pommel.

*Source: Waterdeep: Dungeon of the Mad Mage p. 86*
