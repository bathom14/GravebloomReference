# Gem of Seeing
*Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement)*  


This gem has 3 charges. As a [Magic](rules/5e/actions.md#Magic) action, you can expend 1 charge. For the next 10 minutes, you have [Truesight](rules/5e/senses.md#Truesight) out to 120 feet when you peer through the gem.

The gem regains `dice:1d3|noform|noparens|avg` (`d3`) expended charges daily at dawn.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 264. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
