# Potion of Heroism
*Potion, rare*  

- **Weight**: 0.5 lbs.

When you drink this potion, you gain 10 [Temporary Hit Points](rules/5e/variant-rules/temporary-hit-points-xphb.md) that last for 1 hour. For the same duration, you are under the effect of the [Bless](compendium/5e/spells/bless.md) spell (no [Concentration](rules/5e/conditions.md#Concentration) required).

This potion's blue liquid bubbles and steams as if boiling.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 288. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
