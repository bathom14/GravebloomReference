# Eyes of Minute Seeing
*Wondrous item, uncommon*  


These crystal lenses fit over the eyes. While wearing them, your vision improves significantly out to a range of 1 foot, granting you [Darkvision](rules/5e/senses.md#Darkvision) within that range and [Advantage](rules/5e/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on Intelligence ([Investigation](rules/5e/skills.md#Investigation)) checks made to examine something within that range.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 261. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
