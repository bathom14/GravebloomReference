# Ruin Card
*Wondrous item, legendary*  


As an action, you can brandish this card and use it to cast the [Disintegrate](compendium/5e/spells/disintegrate.md) spell (save DC 17). Once this property is used, it can't be used again until the next dawn.

*Source: The Book of Many Things p. 15*
