# Dragongleam
*Weapon ([spear](compendium/5e/items/spear-phb.md))*  

- **Damage**:
  - One-handed: 1d6 piercing
  - Two-handed: 1d8 piercing
- **Range**: 20/60
- **Properties**: [Thrown](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Thrown), [Versatile](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Versatile)
- **Weight**: 3.0 lbs.

This spear is enchanted with 10 charges of a [daylight](compendium/5e/spells/daylight.md) spell for use in twilight or dark forest underbrush. The command phrase is "Tiamat's eyes shine," written in Draconic runes on the spear's crossguard.

*Source: Hoard of the Dragon Queen p. 84, Tyranny of Dragons p. 84*
