# Periapt of Wound Closure
*Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement)*  

- **Weight**: 1.0 lbs.

While wearing this pendant, you gain the following benefits.

## Life Preservation

Whenever you make a [Death Saving Throw](rules/5e/variant-rules/death-saving-throw-xphb.md), you can change a roll of 9 or lower to a 10, turning a failed save into a successful one.

## Natural Healing Boost

Whenever you roll a [Hit Point Die](rules/5e/variant-rules/hit-point-dice-xphb.md) to regain [Hit Points](rules/5e/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md), double the number of [Hit Points](rules/5e/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) it restores.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 284. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
