# Rakdos Riteknife
*Weapon ([dagger](compendium/5e/items/dagger-phb.md)), legendary (requires attunement)*  

- **Damage**: 1d4 piercing
- **Range**: 20/60
- **Properties**: [Finesse](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Finesse), [Light](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Light), [Thrown](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Thrown)
- **Weight**: 1.0 lbs.

You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Its blade is cruelly serrated, and its hilt resembles a demonic head and wings. Whenever you slay a creature with an attack using the dagger, the creature's soul is imprisoned inside the dagger, and that creature can be restored to life only by a [wish](compendium/5e/spells/wish.md) spell. The dagger can hold a maximum of five souls.

For each soul imprisoned in the dagger, your attacks with it deal an extra `dice:1d4|noform|noparens|avg` (`d4`) necrotic damage on a hit. While the dagger is within 5 feet of you, your dreams are haunted by whispers from the trapped souls.

The dagger has the following additional properties.

## Siphon Vitality

As a bonus action, you can release any number of stored souls from the dagger to regain `dice:1d10|noform|noparens|avg` (`d10`) hit points per soul released.

## Annihilation

If the dagger holds five souls, you can use this property: As a reaction immediately after you hit a creature with the dagger and deal damage to that target, you can release all five souls. If the target now has fewer than 75 hit points, it must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or die. If the target dies, you can't use this property again until you finish a long rest.

*Source: Guildmasters' Guide to Ravnica p. 180*
