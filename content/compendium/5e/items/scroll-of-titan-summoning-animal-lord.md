# Scroll of Titan Summoning (Animal Lord)
*Scroll, legendary*  


When you take a [Magic](rules/5e/actions.md#Magic) action to read this scroll, an [Animal Lord](compendium/5e/bestiary/celestial/animal-lord.md) appears in an unoccupied space on the ground or in water that you can see within 1 mile of yourself.

The [Animal Lord](compendium/5e/bestiary/celestial/animal-lord.md) is [Hostile](rules/5e/variant-rules/hostile-attitude-xphb.md) toward all other creatures and disappears when it drops to 0 [Hit Points](rules/5e/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md). If the [Animal Lord](compendium/5e/bestiary/celestial/animal-lord.md) is summoned into a space that isn't large enough to contain it, the summoning fails, and the scroll is wasted.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 303*
