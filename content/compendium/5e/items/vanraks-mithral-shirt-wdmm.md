# Vanrak's Mithral Shirt
*Medium armor ([chain shirt](compendium/5e/items/chain-shirt-phb.md)), uncommon*  

- **Armor Class**: 13 + Dex modifier (max of +2)
- **Weight**: 20.0 lbs.

Mithral is a light, flexible metal. A mithral chain shirt or breastplate can be worn under normal clothes. If the armor normally imposes disadvantage on Dexterity ([Stealth](rules/5e/skills.md#Stealth)) checks or has a Strength requirement, the mithral version of the armor doesn't.

While you wear this armor, you gain [darkvision](rules/5e/senses.md#Darkvision) out to a range of 60 feet.

*Source: Waterdeep: Dungeon of the Mad Mage p. 237*
