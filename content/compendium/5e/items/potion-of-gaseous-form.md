# Potion of Gaseous Form
*Potion, rare*  

- **Weight**: 0.5 lbs.

When you drink this potion, you gain the effect of the [Gaseous Form](compendium/5e/spells/gaseous-form.md) spell for 1 hour (no [Concentration](rules/5e/conditions.md#Concentration) required) or until you end the effect as a [Bonus Action](rules/5e/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md).

This potion's container seems to hold fog that moves and pours like water.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 287. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
