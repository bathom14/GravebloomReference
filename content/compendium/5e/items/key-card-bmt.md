# Key Card
*Wondrous item, legendary*  


As an action, you can brandish this card and use it to cast the [Knock](compendium/5e/spells/knock.md) spell, with no sound made by the spell. This property can be used five times, and it regains all expended uses at dawn.

*Source: The Book of Many Things p. 14*
