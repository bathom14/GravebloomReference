# Staff of the Ivory Claw
*Staff, weapon, rare (requires attunement by a spellcaster)*  

- **Damage**:
  - One-handed: 1d6 bludgeoning
  - Two-handed: 1d8 bludgeoning
- **Properties**: [Versatile](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Versatile)
- **Weight**: 4.0 lbs.

This gray-and-cerulean staff is topped with a small dragon claw carved from ivory. While holding the staff, you gain a +1 bonus to spell attack rolls. Whenever you score a critical hit with a spell attack, the target takes an extra `dice:3d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`3d6`) radiant damage.

*Source: Explorer's Guide to Wildemount p. 270*
