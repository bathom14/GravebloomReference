# Fane-Eater
*Weapon ([battleaxe](compendium/5e/items/battleaxe-phb.md)), legendary (requires attunement by an evil cleric or paladin)*  

- **Damage**:
  - One-handed: 1d8 slashing
  - Two-handed: 1d10 slashing
- **Properties**: [Versatile](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Versatile)
- **Weight**: 4.0 lbs.

Fane-Eater is a battleaxe belonging to [Arkhan the Cruel](compendium/5e/bestiary/npc/arkhan-the-cruel-bgdia.md).

You gain a +3 bonus to attack and damage rolls with Fane-Eater. If you attack a creature with this weapon and roll a 20 on the attack roll, the creature takes an extra `dice:2d8|noform|noparens|avg` (`2d8`) necrotic damage, and you regain a number of hit points equal to the necrotic damage taken.

*Source: Baldur's Gate: Descent Into Avernus p. 223*
