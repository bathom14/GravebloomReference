# Matalotok
*Weapon ([warhammer](compendium/5e/items/warhammer-phb.md)), legendary (requires attunement)*  

- **Damage**:
  - One-handed: 1d8 bludgeoning
  - Two-handed: 1d10 bludgeoning
- **Range**: 20/60
- **Properties**: [Versatile](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Versatile)
- **Weight**: 2.0 lbs.

Matalotok, the Frost Father, is an ancient hammer fashioned by Thrym, the god of frost giants. The favored weapon of the demon lord [Kostchtchie](compendium/5e/bestiary/npc/kostchtchie-bgdia.md), Matalotok is frigid to the touch and wreathed in mist.

You are immune to cold damage while holding Matalotok. Whenever it deals damage to a creature, the hammer radiates a burst of intense cold in a 30-foot-radius sphere. Each creature in that area takes `dice:3d6|noform|noparens|avg|text(10)` (`3d6`) cold damage.

*Source: Baldur's Gate: Descent Into Avernus p. 224*
