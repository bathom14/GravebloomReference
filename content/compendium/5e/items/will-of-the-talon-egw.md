# Will of the Talon
*Weapon ([war pick](compendium/5e/items/war-pick-phb.md)), artifact (requires attunement)*  

- **Damage**: 1d8 piercing
- **Properties**: [Vestige of Divergence](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Vestige%20of%20Divergence)

Black, blue, green, red, and white gems carved in the form of dragon heads cover this ostentatious gold war pick, marking it as a weapon channeling the power of Tiamat.
