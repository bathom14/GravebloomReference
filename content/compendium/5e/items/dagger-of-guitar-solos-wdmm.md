# Dagger of Guitar Solos
*Weapon ([dagger](compendium/5e/items/dagger-phb.md))*  

- **Damage**: 1d4 piercing
- **Range**: 20/60
- **Properties**: [Finesse](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Finesse), [Light](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Light), [Thrown](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Thrown)
- **Cost**: 750 gp
- **Weight**: 1.0 lbs.

This drow-made dagger is decorated with silver web filigree. It magically plays a fragment of a guitar solo when struck or used to strike a foe.

*Source: Waterdeep: Dungeon of the Mad Mage p. 228*
