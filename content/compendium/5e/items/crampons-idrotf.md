# Crampons
*Adventuring gear*  

- **Cost**: 2 gp
- **Weight**: 0.25 lbs.

A crampon is a metal plate with spikes that is strapped to the sole of a boot. A creature wearing crampons can't fall [prone](rules/5e/conditions.md#Prone) while moving across [slippery ice](compendium/5e/traps-hazards/slippery-ice.md).

*Source: Icewind Dale: Rime of the Frostmaiden p. 20*
