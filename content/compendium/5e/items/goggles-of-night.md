# Goggles of Night
*Wondrous item, uncommon*  


While wearing these dark lenses, you have [Darkvision](rules/5e/senses.md#Darkvision) out to 60 feet. If you already have [Darkvision](rules/5e/senses.md#Darkvision), wearing the goggles increases its range by 60 feet.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 265. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
