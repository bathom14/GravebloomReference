# Birdpipes
*Instrument*  


Pan pipes or satyr pipes, also known as the shalm, these are sacred to [Lliira](compendium/5e/deities/faerunian-lliira-scag.md) and popular with wood elf and wild elf bards.

*Source: Sword Coast Adventurer's Guide p. 124*
