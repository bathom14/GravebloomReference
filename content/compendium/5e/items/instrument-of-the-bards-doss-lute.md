# Instrument of the Bards, Doss Lute
*Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement by a bard)*  


An Instrument of the Bards is superior to an ordinary instrument in every way. Seven types of these instruments exist, each named after a bard college. A creature that attempts to play the instrument without being attuned to it must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or take `dice:2d4|noform|noparens|avg` (`2d4`) Psychic damage.

You can play the Doss [Lute](compendium/5e/items/lute.md) to cast one of the following spells: [Fly](compendium/5e/spells/fly.md), [Invisibility](compendium/5e/spells/invisibility.md), [Levitate](compendium/5e/spells/levitate.md), [Protection from Evil and Good](compendium/5e/spells/protection-from-evil-and-good.md), [Animal Friendship](compendium/5e/spells/animal-friendship.md), [Protection from Energy](compendium/5e/spells/protection-from-energy.md) (Fire damage only), and [Protection from Poison](compendium/5e/spells/protection-from-poison.md). Once the Doss [Lute](compendium/5e/items/lute.md) has been used to cast a spell, it can't be used to cast that spell again until the next dawn. The spells use your spellcasting ability and spell save DC.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 272*
