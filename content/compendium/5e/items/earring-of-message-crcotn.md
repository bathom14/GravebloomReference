# Earring of Message
*Wondrous item, common*  


The blue crystal of this earring is wrapped with delicate copper wire. The earring has 5 charges. While wearing it, you can use an action to expend 1 charge and cast the [message](compendium/5e/spells/message.md) spell. The earring regains `dice:1d4+1|noform|noparens|avg` (`1d4 + 1`) expended charges daily at dawn.

*Source: Critical Role: Call of the Netherdeep p. 212*
