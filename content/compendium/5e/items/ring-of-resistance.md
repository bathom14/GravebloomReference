# Ring of Resistance
*Ring, rare*  


You have [Resistance](rules/5e/variant-rules/resistance-xphb.md) to one damage type while wearing this ring. The gemstone in the ring indicates the type, which the DM chooses or determines randomly by rolling on the following table.
