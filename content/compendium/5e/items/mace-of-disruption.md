# Mace of Disruption
*Weapon ([mace](compendium/5e/items/mace.md)), rare (requires attunement)*  

- **Damage**: 1d6 bludgeoning
- **Weight**: 4.0 lbs.

When you hit a Fiend or an Undead with this magic weapon, that creature takes an extra `dice:2d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`2d6`) Radiant damage. If the target has 25 [Hit Points](rules/5e/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) or fewer after taking this damage, it must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or be destroyed. On a successful save, the creature has the [Frightened](rules/5e/conditions.md#Frightened) condition until the end of your next turn.

## Light

While you hold this weapon, it sheds [Bright Light](rules/5e/variant-rules/bright-light-xphb.md) in a 20-foot radius and [Dim Light](rules/5e/variant-rules/dim-light-xphb.md) for an additional 20 feet.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 276. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
