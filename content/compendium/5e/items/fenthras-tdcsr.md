# Fenthras
*Weapon ([longbow](compendium/5e/items/longbow-phb.md)), legendary (requires attunement)*  

- **Damage**: 1d8 piercing
- **Range**: 150/600
- **Properties**: [Ammunition](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Ammunition), [Heavy](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Heavy), [Two-Handed](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Two-Handed), [Vestige of Divergence](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Vestige%20of%20Divergence)
- **Weight**: 2.0 lbs.

This gorgeous [longbow](compendium/5e/items/longbow-phb.md) made of everbark from the Fey Realm flexes and shifts like a living entity. It is said to have been wielded by the legendary ranger [Vex'ahlia](compendium/5e/bestiary/humanoid/vexahlia-tdcsr.md) against great evils.
