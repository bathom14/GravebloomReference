# Cloak of Invisibility
*Wondrous item, legendary (requires attunement)*  


This cloak has 3 charges and regains `dice:1d3|noform|noparens|avg` (`d3`) expended charges daily at dawn. While wearing the cloak, you can take a [Magic](rules/5e/actions.md#Magic) action to pull its hood over your head and expend 1 charge to give yourself the [Invisible](rules/5e/conditions.md#Invisible) condition for 1 hour. The effect ends early if you pull the hood down (no action required) or cease wearing the hood.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 244. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
