# Potion of Poison
*Potion, uncommon*  

- **Weight**: 0.5 lbs.

This concoction looks, smells, and tastes like a [Potion of Healing](compendium/5e/items/potion-of-healing.md) or another beneficial potion. However, it is actually poison masked by illusion magic. [Identify](compendium/5e/spells/identify.md) reveals its true nature.

If you drink this potion, you take `dice:4d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`4d6`) Poison damage and must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or have the [Poisoned](rules/5e/conditions.md#Poisoned) condition for 1 hour.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 288. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
