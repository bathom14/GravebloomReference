# Reveler's Concertina
*Wondrous item, instrument, rare (requires attunement by a bard)*  


While holding this concertina, you gain a +2 bonus to the saving throw DC of your bard spells.

As an action, you can use the concertina to cast [Otto's irresistible dance](compendium/5e/spells/ottos-irresistible-dance.md) from the item. This property of the concertina can't be used again until the next dawn.

*Source: Tasha's Cauldron of Everything p. 134*
