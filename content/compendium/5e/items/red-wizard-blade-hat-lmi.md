# Red Wizard Blade
*Weapon ([dagger](compendium/5e/items/dagger-phb.md)), legendary*  

- **Damage**: 1d4 piercing
- **Range**: 20/60
- **Properties**: [Finesse](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Finesse), [Light](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Light), [Thrown](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Thrown)
- **Weight**: 1.0 lbs.

Forged by Red Wizards using a secret process known only to them, this grim steel dagger draws its power from the Negative Plane. When you hit a creature with a melee attack using this magic dagger, the target takes an extra `dice:3d12|noform|noparens|avg` (`3d12`) necrotic damage.

A creature reduced to 0 hit points by this weapon dies and can't be raised from the dead except by a deity or by a creature using a [tablet of reawakening](compendium/5e/items/tablet-of-reawakening-hat-lmi.md) to cast the [true resurrection](compendium/5e/spells/true-resurrection.md) spell.

*Source: Honor Among Thieves: Legendary Magic Items*
