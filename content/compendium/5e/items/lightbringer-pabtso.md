# Lightbringer
*Weapon ([mace](compendium/5e/items/mace-phb.md)), uncommon*  

- **Damage**: 1d6 bludgeoning
- **Weight**: 4.0 lbs.

You have a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

This weapon, known as Lightbringer, was made for a cleric of Lathander, the god of dawn. The head of the mace is shaped like a sunburst and is made of solid brass. The mace glows as bright as a torch when its wielder commands. While glowing, the mace deals an extra `dice:1d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`d6`) radiant damage to Undead creatures.

*Source: Phandelver and Below: The Shattered Obelisk p. 72*
