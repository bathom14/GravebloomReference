# Sylvan Talon
*Common (requires attunement)*  


While this weapon is on your person, you understand the non-written communication of all Fey, and they understand yours.

## Secret Message

As a [Magic](rules/5e/actions.md#Magic) action, you can use the weapon to cast [Message](compendium/5e/spells/message.md). Once this property is used, it can't be used again until the next dawn.
