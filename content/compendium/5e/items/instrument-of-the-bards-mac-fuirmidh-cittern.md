# Instrument of the Bards, Mac-Fuirmidh Cittern
*Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement by a bard)*  


An Instrument of the Bards is superior to an ordinary instrument in every way. Seven types of these instruments exist, each named after a bard college. A creature that attempts to play the instrument without being attuned to it must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or take `dice:2d4|noform|noparens|avg` (`2d4`) Psychic damage.

You can play the Mac-Fuirmidh Cittern to cast one of the following spells: [Fly](compendium/5e/spells/fly.md), [Invisibility](compendium/5e/spells/invisibility.md), [Levitate](compendium/5e/spells/levitate.md), [Protection from Evil and Good](compendium/5e/spells/protection-from-evil-and-good.md), [Barkskin](compendium/5e/spells/barkskin.md), [Cure Wounds](compendium/5e/spells/cure-wounds.md), and [Fog Cloud](compendium/5e/spells/fog-cloud.md). Once the Mac-Fuirmidh Cittern has been used to cast a spell, it can't be used to cast that spell again until the next dawn. The spells use your spellcasting ability and spell save DC.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 272*
