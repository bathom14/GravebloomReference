# Bloodrage Greataxe
*Weapon ([greataxe](compendium/5e/items/greataxe-phb.md)), uncommon*

- **Properties**: [Heavy](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Heavy), [Two-Handed](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Two-Handed)
