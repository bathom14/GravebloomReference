# Emerald Pen
*Wondrous item, uncommon*  


This pen is tipped with an emerald nib and requires no ink to write. While holding this pen, you can cast [illusory script](compendium/5e/spells/illusory-script.md) at will, requiring no material components.

*Source: Fizban's Treasury of Dragons p. 23*
