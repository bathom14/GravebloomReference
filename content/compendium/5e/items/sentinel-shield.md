# Sentinel Shield
*Armor ([shield](compendium/5e/items/shield-phb.md)), uncommon*  

- **Armor Class**: 2
- **Weight**: 6.0 lbs.

While holding this Shield, you have [Advantage](rules/5e/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on [Initiative](rules/5e/variant-rules/initiative-xphb.md) rolls and Wisdom ([Perception](rules/5e/skills.md#Perception)) checks. The Shield is emblazoned with a symbol of an eye.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 303. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
