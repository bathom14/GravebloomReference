# Black Sap
*Adventuring gear*  

- **Cost**: 300 gp

This tarry substance harvested from the dark boughs of the death's head willow is a powerful intoxicant. It can be smoked as a concentrate or injected directly into the bloodstream. A creature subjected to a dose of black sap cannot be [charmed](rules/5e/conditions.md#Charmed) or [frightened](rules/5e/conditions.md#Frightened) for `dice:1d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`d6`) hours.

For each dose of black sap consumed, a creature must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or become [poisoned](rules/5e/conditions.md#Poisoned) for `dice:2d4|noform|noparens|avg` (`2d4`) hours—an effect that is cumulative with multiple doses.

*Source: Explorer's Guide to Wildemount p. 152*
