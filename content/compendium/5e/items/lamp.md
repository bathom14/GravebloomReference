# Lamp
*Adventuring gear*  

- **Cost**: 5 sp
- **Weight**: 1.0 lbs.

A Lamp burns [Oil](compendium/5e/items/oil.md) as fuel to cast [Bright Light](rules/5e/variant-rules/bright-light-xphb.md) in a 15-foot radius and [Dim Light](rules/5e/variant-rules/dim-light-xphb.md) for an additional 30 feet.

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 226. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
