# Puzzle Card
*Wondrous item, legendary*  


As an action, you can hold this card aloft and magically disable each trap within 60 feet of you that isn't behind total cover. A trap, for the purpose of this property, includes any object or mechanism that would inflict a sudden effect that was intended by its maker to cause harm or inconvenience. If the trap is the effect of a spell, such as [Alarm](compendium/5e/spells/alarm.md), that trap is suppressed for 10 minutes instead of being disabled. Once this property is used, it can't be used again until the next dawn.

*Source: The Book of Many Things p. 14*
