# Crystal Ball of Mind Reading
*Wondrous item, legendary (requires attunement)*  

- **Weight**: 3.0 lbs.

While touching this crystal orb, you can cast [Scrying](compendium/5e/spells/scrying.md) (save DC 17) with it. In addition, you can cast [Detect Thoughts](compendium/5e/spells/detect-thoughts.md) (save DC 17) targeting creatures you can see within 30 feet of the spell's sensor. You don't need to concentrate on this [Detect Thoughts](compendium/5e/spells/detect-thoughts.md) spell to maintain it during its duration, but it ends if the [Scrying](compendium/5e/spells/scrying.md) spell ends.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 159. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
