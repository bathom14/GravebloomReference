# Plate of the Dawnmartyr
*Heavy armor ([plate armor](compendium/5e/items/plate-armor-phb.md)), legendary (requires attunement)*  

- **Armor Class**: 18
- **Properties**: [Vestige of Divergence](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Vestige%20of%20Divergence)
- **Strength**: Requires 15 STR.
- **Stealth**: The wearer has disadvantage on Stealth (DEX) checks.
- **Weight**: 65.0 lbs.

Beautiful silver scrollwork and priceless cut rubies adorn this set of brass-and-gold armor. Legend holds that it was worn by Duana, High Priest of the [The Dawnfather](compendium/5e/deities/exandria-the-dawnfather-tdcsr.md), when she was martyred in the Battle of Ghor Dranas in the final days of the "Calamity". In more recent times, it was worn by [Pike Trickfoot](compendium/5e/bestiary/npc/pike-trickfoot-tdcsr.md), a legendary cleric of the [The Everlight](compendium/5e/deities/exandria-the-everlight-tdcsr.md), while fighting against "the Chroma Conclave" and [The Whispered One](compendium/5e/deities/exandria-the-whispered-one-tdcsr.md).
