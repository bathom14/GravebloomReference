# Weapon of Throne's Command
*Very rare (requires attunement)*  


This weapon is bedecked in ornate gold filigree and deep-blue and maroon jewels. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this weapon. Additionally, you gain proficiency in the [Intimidation](rules/5e/skills.md#Intimidation) and [Persuasion](rules/5e/skills.md#Persuasion) skills if you don't already have it.

## Spellcasting

The weapon has 5 charges. You can use a bonus action and expend 1 or more of its charges to cast one of the following spells (save DC 16): [Command](compendium/5e/spells/command.md) (1 charge), [Zone of Truth](compendium/5e/spells/zone-of-truth.md) (2 charges), [Compulsion](compendium/5e/spells/compulsion.md) (4 charges), [Banishment](compendium/5e/spells/banishment.md) (4 charges), or [Dominate Person](compendium/5e/spells/dominate-person.md) (5 charges).

The weapon regains `dice:1d4|noform|noparens|avg` (`d4`) expended charges daily at dawn.
