# Jester Card
*Wondrous item, legendary*  


As an action, you can brandish this card and use it to cast the [Otto's Irresistible Dance](compendium/5e/spells/ottos-irresistible-dance.md) spell (save DC 17), and you have advantage on Constitution saving throws to maintain [concentration](rules/5e/conditions.md#Concentration) on it. Once this property is used, it can't be used again until the next dawn.

*Source: The Book of Many Things p. 14*
