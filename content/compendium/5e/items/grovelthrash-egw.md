# Grovelthrash
*Weapon ([warhammer](compendium/5e/items/warhammer-phb.md)), artifact (requires attunement)*  

- **Damage**:
  - One-handed: 1d8 bludgeoning
  - Two-handed: 1d10 bludgeoning
- **Properties**: [Versatile](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Versatile), [Vestige of Divergence](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Vestige%20of%20Divergence)
