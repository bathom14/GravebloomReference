# Condemner
*Weapon ([heavy crossbow](compendium/5e/items/heavy-crossbow-phb.md)), legendary (requires attunement)*  

- **Damage**: 1d10 piercing
- **Range**: 100/400
- **Properties**: [Ammunition](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Ammunition), [Heavy](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Heavy), [Loading](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Loading), [Two-Handed](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Two-Handed), [Vestige of Divergence](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Vestige%20of%20Divergence)
- **Weight**: 18.0 lbs.

This shadow-touched [heavy crossbow](compendium/5e/items/heavy-crossbow-phb.md) once served as the weapon of choice for the legendary assassin Todora. Though few know this, Condemner is now in the possession of an equally infamous assassin named "Ixrattu Khar".
