# Gunpowder (keg)
*Explosive*  

- **Cost**: 250 gp
- **Weight**: 20.0 lbs.

Setting fire to a keg full of Gunpowder causes it to explode. When a keg explodes, each creature in a 10-foot-radius [Sphere](rules/5e/variant-rules/sphere-area-of-effect-xphb.md) centered on the keg makes a DC 12 Dexterity saving throw, taking `dice:7d6|noform|noparens|avg|text(24)` (`7d6`) Fire damage on a failed save or half as much damage on a successful one.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 73*
