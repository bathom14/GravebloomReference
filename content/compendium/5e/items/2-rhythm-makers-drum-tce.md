# +2 Rhythm-Maker's Drum
*Wondrous item, instrument, rare (requires attunement by a bard)*  

- **Weight**: 3.0 lbs.

While holding this [drum](compendium/5e/items/drum-phb.md), you gain a +2 bonus to spell attack rolls and to the saving throw DCs of your bard spells.

As an action, you can play the [drum](compendium/5e/items/drum-phb.md) to regain one use of your Bardic Inspiration feature. This property of the [drum](compendium/5e/items/drum-phb.md) can't be used again until the next dawn.

*Source: Tasha's Cauldron of Everything p. 134*
