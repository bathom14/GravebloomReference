# Vial of Stardust



Any creature that sprinkles the contents of a Vial of Stardust over itself gains the ability to cast the [dream](compendium/5e/spells/dream.md) spell once as an action (spell save DC 15), requiring no components.

*Source: Waterdeep: Dungeon of the Mad Mage p. 251*
