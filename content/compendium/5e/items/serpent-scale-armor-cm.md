# Serpent Scale Armor
*Medium armor ([scale mail](compendium/5e/items/scale-mail-phb.md)), uncommon*  

- **Armor Class**: 14 + Dex modifier (max of +2)
- **Weight**: 45.0 lbs.

This suit of magic armor is made from shimmering scales. While wearing it, you can apply your full Dexterity modifier (instead of a maximum of +2) when determining your Armor Class. In addition, this armor does not impose disadvantage on your Dexterity ([Stealth](rules/5e/skills.md#Stealth)) checks.

*Source: Candlekeep Mysteries p. 98*
