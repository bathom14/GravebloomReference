# Balance Card
*Wondrous item, legendary*  


As an action, you can brandish this card at one creature you can see within 30 feet of yourself and drain its life force. The target must succeed on a DC 17 Constitution saving throw or take `dice:4d8|noform|noparens|avg` (`4d8`) necrotic damage. You then regain a number of hit points equal to the necrotic damage the target takes. Once this property is used, it can't be used again until the next dawn."

*Source: The Book of Many Things p. 13*
