# Dust of Dryness
*Wondrous item, uncommon*  


This small packet contains `dice:1d6+4|noform|noparens|avg` (`1d6 + 4`) pinches of dust. As a [Utilize](rules/5e/actions.md#Utilize) action, you can sprinkle a pinch of the dust over water, turning up to a 15-foot [Cube](rules/5e/variant-rules/cube-area-of-effect-xphb.md) of water into one marble-sized pellet, which floats or rests near where the dust was sprinkled. The pellet's weight is negligible. A creature can take a [Utilize](rules/5e/actions.md#Utilize) action to smash the pellet against a hard surface, causing the pellet to shatter and release the water the dust absorbed. Doing so destroys the pellet and ends its magic.

As a [Utilize](rules/5e/actions.md#Utilize) action, you can sprinkle a pinch of the dust on an Elemental within 5 feet of yourself that is composed mostly of water (such as a [Water Elemental](compendium/5e/bestiary/elemental/water-elemental.md) or a [Water Weird](compendium/5e/bestiary/elemental/water-weird.md)). Such a creature exposed to a pinch of the dust makes a DC 13 Constitution saving throw, taking `dice:10d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`10d6`) Necrotic damage on a failed save or half as much damage on a successful one.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 255. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
