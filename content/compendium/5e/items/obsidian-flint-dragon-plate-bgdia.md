# Obsidian Flint Dragon Plate
*Heavy armor ([plate armor](compendium/5e/items/plate-armor-phb.md)), legendary*  

- **Armor Class**: 18
- **Strength**: Requires 15 STR.
- **Stealth**: The wearer has disadvantage on Stealth (DEX) checks.
- **Weight**: 65.0 lbs.

You gain a +2 bonus to AC and resistance to poison damage while you wear this armor. In addition, you gain advantage on ability checks and saving throws made to avoid or end the [grappled](rules/5e/conditions.md#Grappled) condition on yourself.

*Source: Baldur's Gate: Descent Into Avernus p. 224*
