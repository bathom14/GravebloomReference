# Skull Card
*Wondrous item, legendary*  


As an action, you can brandish this card and use it to cast [Spirit of Death](compendium/5e/spells/spirit-of-death-bmt.md) (see "chapter 7") as a 6th-level spell (spell attack bonus `dice:1d20+9|noform|noparens|text(+9)`, save DC 17). Once this property is used, it can't be used again until the next dawn.

*Source: The Book of Many Things p. 15*
