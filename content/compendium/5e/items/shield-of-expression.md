# Shield of Expression
*Armor ([shield](compendium/5e/items/shield-phb.md)), common*  

- **Armor Class**: 2
- **Weight**: 6.0 lbs.

The front of this Shield is shaped in the likeness of a face. While bearing the Shield, you can take a [Bonus Action](rules/5e/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) to alter the face's expression.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 303*
