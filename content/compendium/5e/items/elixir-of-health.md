# Elixir of Health
*Potion, rare*  

- **Weight**: 0.5 lbs.

When you drink this potion, you are cured of all magical contagions. In addition, the following conditions end on you: [Blinded](rules/5e/conditions.md#Blinded), [Deafened](rules/5e/conditions.md#Deafened), [Paralyzed](rules/5e/conditions.md#Paralyzed), and [Poisoned](rules/5e/conditions.md#Poisoned).

The clear, red liquid has tiny bubbles of light in it.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 257. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
