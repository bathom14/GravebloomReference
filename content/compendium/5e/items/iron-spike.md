# Iron Spike
*Adventuring gear*  

- **Cost**: 1 sp
- **Weight**: 0.5 lbs.

Iron Spikes come in bundles of ten. As a [Utilize](rules/5e/actions.md#Utilize) action, you can use a blunt object, such as a [Light Hammer](compendium/5e/items/light-hammer.md), to hammer a spike into wood, earth, or a similar material. You can do so to jam a door shut or to then tie a [Rope](compendium/5e/items/rope.md) or [Chain](compendium/5e/items/chain.md) to the Spike.

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 228. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
