# Cracked Driftglobe
*Wondrous item, uncommon*  

- **Weight**: 1.0 lbs.

This small sphere of thick glass weighs 1 pound. If you are within 60 feet of it, you can speak its command word and cause it to emanate the [light](compendium/5e/spells/light.md) spell.

*Source: Candlekeep Mysteries p. 110*
