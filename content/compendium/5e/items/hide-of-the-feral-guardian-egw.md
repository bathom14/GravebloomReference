# Hide of the Feral Guardian
*Light armor ([studded leather armor](compendium/5e/items/studded-leather-armor-phb.md)), legendary (requires attunement)*  

- **Armor Class**: 12 + Dex modifier
- **Properties**: [Vestige of Divergence](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Vestige%20of%20Divergence)
- **Weight**: 13.0 lbs.

It is believed that this polished and beautifully detailed leather armor was a gift from Melora, bestowed on a long-forgotten archdruid and champion of the natural world before the terrors of the Calamity.
