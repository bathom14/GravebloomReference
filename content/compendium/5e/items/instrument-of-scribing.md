# Instrument of Scribing
*Wondrous item, common*  


This [musical instrument](compendium/5e/items/musical-instrument.md) has 3 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. While you are playing it, you can take a [Magic](rules/5e/actions.md#Magic) action to expend 1 charge and write a magical message on a nonmagical object or surface that you can see within 30 feet of yourself. The message can be up to six words long and is written in a language you know. If you are a Bard, you can scribe an additional seven words and make the message glow faintly, allowing it to be seen in nonmagical [Darkness](rules/5e/variant-rules/darkness-xphb.md). Casting the [Dispel Magic](compendium/5e/spells/dispel-magic.md) spell on the message erases it. Otherwise, the message fades away after 24 hours.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 271*
