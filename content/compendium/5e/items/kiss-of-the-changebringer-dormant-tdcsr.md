# Kiss of the Changebringer (Dormant)
*Wondrous item, legendary (requires attunement)*  

- **Properties**: [Vestige of Divergence](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Vestige%20of%20Divergence)

A beautiful, glittering emerald is set within this silver chain, humming with power and possibility. It is said that this amulet was stolen from the neck of [The Changebringer](compendium/5e/deities/exandria-the-changebringer-tdcsr.md) herself by the [The Lord of the Hells](compendium/5e/deities/exandria-the-lord-of-the-hells-tdcsr.md), and locked within the vaults of Bazzoxan in "Wildemount". It was later claimed there by a group of Aurora Watch soldiers during an expedition into that grim fortress's abandoned temples.

## Dormant

While the Kiss of the Changebringer is in a dormant state, you have a +1 bonus to saving throws and are immune to the [grappled](rules/5e/conditions.md#Grappled) condition while wearing it.

*Source: Tal'Dorei Campaign Setting Reborn p. 206*
