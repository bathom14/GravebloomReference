# Tidecaller Trident
*Weapon ([trident](compendium/5e/items/trident-phb.md)), very rare (requires attunement)*  

- **Damage**:
  - One-handed: 1d6 piercing
  - Two-handed: 1d8 piercing
- **Range**: 20/60
- **Properties**: [Thrown](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Thrown), [Versatile](rules/5e/item-properties.md#Versatile)
- **Weight**: 4.0 lbs.

You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic trident. You also have advantage on attack rolls made with this weapon while underwater.

This trident has 3 charges. You can expend 1 charge to cast [Control Water](compendium/5e/spells/control-water.md) (save DC 15) from the trident or 3 charges to cast [Tsunami](compendium/5e/spells/tsunami.md) (save DC 15) from it instead. The trident regains `dice:1d3|noform|noparens|avg` (`d3`) expended charges daily at dawn.

*Source: The Book of Many Things p. 69*
