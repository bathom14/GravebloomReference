# Helm of Underwater Action
*Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement)*  


While wearing this brass helmet, you can breathe underwater, you gain [darkvision](rules/5e/senses.md#Darkvision) with a range of 60 feet, and you gain a swimming speed of 30 feet.

*Source: Ghosts of Saltmarsh p. 229*
