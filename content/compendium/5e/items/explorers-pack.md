# Explorer's Pack
*Adventuring gear*  

- **Cost**: 10 gp
- **Weight**: 55.0 lbs.

An Explorer's Pack contains the following items: [Backpack](compendium/5e/items/backpack.md), [Bedroll](compendium/5e/items/bedroll.md), 2 flasks of [Oil](compendium/5e/items/oil.md), 10 days of [Rations](compendium/5e/items/rations.md), [Rope](compendium/5e/items/rope.md), [Tinderbox](compendium/5e/items/tinderbox.md), 10 [Torches](compendium/5e/items/torch.md), and [Waterskin](compendium/5e/items/waterskin.md).
