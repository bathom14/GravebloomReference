# Ring of Invisibility
*Ring, legendary (requires attunement)*  


While wearing this ring, you can take a [Magic](rules/5e/actions.md#Magic) action to give yourself the [Invisible](rules/5e/conditions.md#Invisible) condition. You remain [Invisible](rules/5e/conditions.md#Invisible) until the ring is removed or until you take a [Bonus Action](rules/5e/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) to become visible again.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 293. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
