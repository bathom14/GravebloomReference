## Traits

***Transparent.*** Even when an ooze-folk is in plain sight, it takes a DC 12 Wisdom ([Perception](rules/5e/skills.md#Perception)) check to spot an ooze-folk that has neither moved nor attacked. A creature that tries to enter the ooze-folk's space while unaware of the ooze-folk is [surprised](rules/5e/conditions.md#Surprised) by the ooze-folk.

***Fragile Bones.*** For each 5 damage it takes, the ooze-folk's walking speed is reduced by 5 feet.

## Actions

***Multiattack.*** The ooze folk makes one glass longsword attack and one pseudopod attack.

***Glass Longsword.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* `dice:1d20+4|noform|noparens|text(+4)` to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. *Hit:* `dice:1d8+2|noform|noparens|avg|text(6)` (`1d8 + 2`) slashing damage. If a 1 is rolled on an attack roll with a glass longsword, it shatters and can no longer be used.

***Pseudopod.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* `dice:1d20+4|noform|noparens|text(+4)` to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. *Hit:* `dice:1d6+2|noform|noparens|avg|text(5)` (`1d6 + 2`) acid damage.
