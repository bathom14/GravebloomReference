# Refraction of Ilvaash
*Source: Phandelver and Below: The Shattered Obelisk p. 197*  

Ilvaash was formed from the remains of the mind flayer god Ilsensine, the God-Brain. When Ilsensine left the Far Realm to establish a divine domain elsewhere, pieces of the God-Brain sloughed away and awoke to sentience. This is Ilvaash, the Dissonant Psyche.

The refraction of Ilvaash wields only a sliver of the godlet's power but is nevertheless a formidable foe.

## Traits

***Creature Sense.*** The refraction is aware of creatures within 100 miles of it that have an Intelligence score of 4 or higher. It knows the distance and direction to each creature, as well as each one's Intelligence score, but can't sense anything else about it. A creature protected by a [mind blank](compendium/5e/spells/mind-blank.md) spell, a [nondetection](compendium/5e/spells/nondetection.md) spell, or similar magic can't be perceived in this manner.

***Incorporeal Movement.*** The refraction can move through other creatures and objects as if they were difficult terrain. It takes `dice:1d10|noform|noparens|avg|text(5)` (`d10`) force damage if it ends its turn inside an object.

***Legendary Resistance (5/Day).*** If the refraction fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.

***Magic Resistance.*** The refraction has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

## Actions

***Multiattack.*** The refraction makes two Dissonant Claw attacks.

***Dissonant Claw.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* `dice:1d20+11|noform|noparens|text(+11)` to hit, reach 10 ft. or range 120 ft., one creature. *Hit:* `dice:3d12+6|noform|noparens|avg|text(25)` (`3d12 + 6`) psychic damage. If the target is a creature [concentrating](rules/5e/conditions.md#Concentration) on a spell, its [concentration](rules/5e/conditions.md#Concentration) is broken.

***Mind Blast (Recharge 5-6).*** Creatures of the refraction's choice within 60 feet of it must succeed on a DC 19 Intelligence saving throw or take `dice:5d10+6|noform|noparens|avg|text(33)` (`5d10 + 6`) psychic damage and have the [stunned](rules/5e/conditions.md#Stunned) condition for 1 minute. A [stunned](rules/5e/conditions.md#Stunned) creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.

***Teleport.*** The refraction teleports, along with any equipment it is wearing or carrying, up to 120 feet to an unoccupied place that it can see.

***Spellcasting (Psionics).*** The refraction casts one of the following spells, requiring no spell components and using Intelligence as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 19):

**At will:** [detect magic](compendium/5e/spells/detect-magic.md), [detect thoughts](compendium/5e/spells/detect-thoughts.md)

**3/day each:** [dispel magic](compendium/5e/spells/dispel-magic.md), [modify memory](compendium/5e/spells/modify-memory.md)

**1/day each:** [feeblemind](compendium/5e/spells/befuddlement.md), [plane shift](compendium/5e/spells/plane-shift.md) (self only)

## Legendary Actions
