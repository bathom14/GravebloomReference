## Traits

***Innate Spellcasting.*** The naiad's spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 14). It can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components:

**At will:** [minor illusion](compendium/5e/spells/minor-illusion.md)

**3/day:** [phantasmal force](compendium/5e/spells/phantasmal-force.md)

**1/day each:** [fly](compendium/5e/spells/fly.md), [hypnotic pattern](compendium/5e/spells/hypnotic-pattern.md)

***Amphibious.*** The naiad can breathe air and water.

***Invisible in Water.*** The naiad is [invisible](rules/5e/conditions.md#Invisible) while fully immersed in water.

***Magic Resistance.*** The naiad has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

## Actions

***Multiattack.*** The naiad makes two psychic touch attacks.

***Psychic Touch.*** *Melee Spell Attack:* `dice:1d20+6|noform|noparens|text(+6)` to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* `dice:1d10+4|noform|noparens|avg|text(9)` (`1d10 + 4`) psychic damage.
