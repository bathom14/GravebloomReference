## Traits

***Antimagic Susceptibility.*** The dagger is [incapacitated](rules/5e/conditions.md#Incapacitated) while in the area of an [antimagic field](compendium/5e/spells/antimagic-field.md). If targeted by [dispel magic](compendium/5e/spells/dispel-magic.md), the dagger must succeed on a Constitution saving throw against the caster's spell save DC or fall [unconscious](rules/5e/conditions.md#Unconscious) for 1 minute.

***False Appearance.*** While the dagger remains motionless and isn't flying, it is indistinguishable from a normal dagger.
