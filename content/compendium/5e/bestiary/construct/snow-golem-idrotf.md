# Snow Golem
*Source: Icewind Dale: Rime of the Frostmaiden p. 308*  

A snow golem is a mass of snow brought to life through magic. Nonmagical weapons pass through its snowy form without dealing any appreciable damage to it, though heat is its doom.

## Traits

***Cold Absorption.*** Whenever the golem is subjected to cold damage, it takes no damage and instead regains a number of hit points equal to the cold damage dealt.

***Immutable Form.*** The golem is immune to any spell or effect that would alter its form.

***Melt.*** While in an area of extreme heat, the golem loses `dice:1d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`d6`) hit points at the start of each of its turns.

***Unusual Nature.*** The golem doesn't require air, food, drink, or sleep.

## Actions

***Multiattack.*** The golem makes three melee attacks.

***Slam.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* `dice:1d20+4|noform|noparens|text(+4)` to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* `dice:1d6+2|noform|noparens|avg|text(5)` (`1d6 + 2`) bludgeoning damage plus `dice:2d6|noform|noparens|avg|text(7)` (`2d6`) cold damage.

***Snowball.*** *Ranged Weapon Attack:* `dice:1d20+0|noform|noparens|text(+0)` to hit, range 60 ft., one target. *Hit:* `dice:2d6+2|noform|noparens|avg|text(9)` (`2d6 + 2`) cold damage.
