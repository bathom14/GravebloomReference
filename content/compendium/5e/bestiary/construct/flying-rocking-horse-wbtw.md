## Traits

***False Appearance.*** If the rocking horse is motionless at the start of combat, it has advantage on its initiative roll. Moreover, if a creature hasn't observed the rocking horse move or act, that creature must succeed on a DC 18 Intelligence ([Investigation](rules/5e/skills.md#Investigation)) check to discern that the rocking horse is animate.

***Flying Mount.*** The rocking horse can serve as a mount for a Medium or smaller creature and can fly only while mounted.

***Unusual Nature.*** The rocking horse doesn't require air, food, drink, or sleep, and it regains no hit points or Hit Dice at the end of a long rest.
