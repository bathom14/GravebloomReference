## Traits

***Swarm.*** The swarm can occupy another creature's space and vice versa, and the swarm can move through any opening large enough for a Tiny book. The swarm can't regain hit points or gain temporary hit points.

## Actions

***Slam.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* `dice:1d20+4|noform|noparens|text(+4)` to hit, reach 0 ft., one creature in the swarm's space. *Hit:* `dice:2d4|noform|noparens|avg|text(5)` (`2d4`) bludgeoning damage, or `dice:1d4|noform|noparens|avg|text(2)` (`d4`) bludgeoning damage if the swarm has half of its hit points or fewer.
