## Traits

***Pounce.*** If the deinonychus moves at least 20 feet straight toward a creature and then hits it with a Claw attack on the same turn, that target must succeed on a DC 12 Strength saving throw or be knocked [prone](rules/5e/conditions.md#Prone). If the target is [prone](rules/5e/conditions.md#Prone), the deinonychus can make one Bite attack against it as a bonus action.

## Actions

***Multiattack.*** The deinonychus makes one Bite attack and two Claw attacks.

***Bite.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* `dice:1d20+4|noform|noparens|text(+4)` to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* `dice:1d8+2|noform|noparens|avg|text(6)` (`1d8 + 2`) piercing damage.
