## Traits

***Water Breathing.*** The octopus can breathe only underwater. It can hold its breath for 1 hour outside water.

## Actions

***Tentacles.*** *Melee Attack Roll:* `dice:1d20+5|noform|noparens|text(+5)`, reach 10 ft. *Hit:* `dice:2d6+3|noform|noparens|avg|text(10)` (`2d6 + 3`) Bludgeoning damage. If the target is a Medium or smaller creature, it has the [Grappled](rules/5e/conditions.md#Grappled) condition (escape DC 13) from all eight tentacles. While [Grappled](rules/5e/conditions.md#Grappled), the target has the [Restrained](rules/5e/conditions.md#Restrained) condition.
