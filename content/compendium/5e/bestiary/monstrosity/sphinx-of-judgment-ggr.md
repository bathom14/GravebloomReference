## Traits

***Spellcasting.*** The sphinx is a 9th-level spellcaster. Its spellcasting ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 16, `dice:1d20+8|noform|noparens|text(+8)` to hit with spell attacks). It requires no material components to cast its spells. The sphinx has the following wizard spells prepared:

**Cantrips (at will):** [friends](compendium/5e/spells/friends.md), [mage hand](compendium/5e/spells/mage-hand.md), [message](compendium/5e/spells/message.md)

**1st level (4 slots):** [command](compendium/5e/spells/command.md), [ensnaring strike](compendium/5e/spells/ensnaring-strike.md), [shield](compendium/5e/spells/shield.md)

**2nd level (3 slots):** [calm emotions](compendium/5e/spells/calm-emotions.md), [hold person](compendium/5e/spells/hold-person.md), [suggestion](compendium/5e/spells/suggestion.md)

**3rd level (3 slots):** [clairvoyance](compendium/5e/spells/clairvoyance.md), [counterspell](compendium/5e/spells/counterspell.md), [dispel magic](compendium/5e/spells/dispel-magic.md)

**4th level (3 slots):** [banishment](compendium/5e/spells/banishment.md), [divination](compendium/5e/spells/divination.md)

**5th level (1 slots):** [dominate person](compendium/5e/spells/dominate-person.md)

***Inscrutable.*** The sphinx is immune to any effect that would sense its emotions or read its thoughts, as well as any divination spell that it refuses. Wisdom ([Insight](rules/5e/skills.md#Insight)) checks made to ascertain the sphinx's intentions or sincerity have disadvantage.

***Magic Weapons.*** The sphinx's weapon attacks are magical.

## Actions

***Multiattack.*** The sphinx makes two claw attacks.

***Claw.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* `dice:1d20+8|noform|noparens|text(+8)` to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* `dice:2d8+4|noform|noparens|avg|text(13)` (`2d8 + 4`) slashing damage.

## Legendary Actions

***Claw Attack.*** The sphinx makes one claw attack.

***Teleport (Costs 2 Actions).*** The sphinx magically teleports, along with any equipment it is wearing or carrying, up to 120 feet to an unoccupied space it can see.

***Cast a Spell (Costs 3 Actions).*** The sphinx casts a spell from its list of prepared spells, using a spell slot as normal.
