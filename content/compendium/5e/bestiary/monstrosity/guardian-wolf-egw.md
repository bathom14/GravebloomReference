## Traits

***Keen Hearing and Smell.*** The wolf has advantage on Wisdom ([Perception](rules/5e/skills.md#Perception)) checks that rely on hearing or smell.

***Pack Tactics.*** The wolf has advantage on attack rolls against a creature if at least one of the wolf's allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally isn't [incapacitated](rules/5e/conditions.md#Incapacitated).

## Actions

***Multiattack.*** The wolf makes two attacks: one with its bite and one with its claws.

***Bite.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* `dice:1d20+8|noform|noparens|text(+8)` to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* `dice:1d10+6|noform|noparens|avg|text(11)` (`1d10 + 6`) piercing damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 16 Strength saving throw or be knocked [prone](rules/5e/conditions.md#Prone).

***Claws.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* `dice:1d20+8|noform|noparens|text(+8)` to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* `dice:2d8+6|noform|noparens|avg|text(15)` (`2d8 + 6`) piercing damage.
