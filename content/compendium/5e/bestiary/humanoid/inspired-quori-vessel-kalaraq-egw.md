## Traits

***Innate Spellcasting (Psionics).*** The Inspired's spellcasting ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 13). It can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components:

**At will:** [mage hand](compendium/5e/spells/mage-hand.md), [vicious mockery](compendium/5e/spells/vicious-mockery.md) (see "Actions" below)

**3/day each:** [arcane eye](compendium/5e/spells/arcane-eye.md)

**1/day each:** [charm person](compendium/5e/spells/charm-person.md), [dissonant whispers](compendium/5e/spells/dissonant-whispers.md), [hex](compendium/5e/spells/hex.md), [hold person](compendium/5e/spells/hold-person.md), [mage armor](compendium/5e/spells/mage-armor.md)

***Dual Mind.*** The Inspired has advantage on Wisdom saving throws.

***All Around Vision.*** The Inspired can't be [surprised](rules/5e/conditions.md#Surprised).

## Actions

***Multiattack.*** The Inspired makes two crysteel dagger attacks. It can replace one attack with vicious mockery.

***Crysteel Dagger.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* `dice:1d20+4|noform|noparens|text(+4)` to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* `dice:1d4+2|noform|noparens|avg|text(4)` (`1d4 + 2`) piercing damage plus `dice:3d6|noform|noparens|avg|text(10)` (`3d6`) force damage.

***Vicious Mockery (Cantrip).*** The Inspired unleashes a string of insults laced with subtle enchantments at one creature it can see within 60 feet of it. If the target can hear the Inspired, the target must succeed on a DC 13 Wisdom saving throw or take `dice:1d4|noform|noparens|avg|text(2)` (`d4`) psychic damage and have disadvantage on the next attack roll it makes before the end of its next turn.
