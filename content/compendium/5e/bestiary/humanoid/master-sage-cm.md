## Actions

***Shocking Grasp (Cantrip).*** *Melee Spell Attack:* `dice:1d20+8|noform|noparens|text(+8)` to hit (with advantage if the target is wearing armor made of metal), reach 5 ft., one creature. *Hit:* `dice:3d8|noform|noparens|avg|text(13)` (`3d8`) lightning damage, and the target can't take reactions until the start of its next turn.

***Fireball (3rd-Level Spell; 3/Day).*** The sage creates a fiery explosion centered on a point it can see within 150 feet of it. Each creature in a 20-foot-radius sphere centered on that point must make a DC 14 Dexterity saving throw, taking `dice:8d6|noform|noparens|avg|text(28)` (`8d6`) fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. The fire spreads around corners and ignites flammable objects in the area that aren't being worn or carried.

***Spellcasting.*** The sage casts one of the following spells, using Intelligence as the spellcasting ability (save DC 14, `dice:1d20+6|noform|noparens|text(+6)` to hit with spell attacks):

**At will:** [light](compendium/5e/spells/light.md), [mage hand](compendium/5e/spells/mage-hand.md), [mending](compendium/5e/spells/mending.md), [prestidigitation](compendium/5e/spells/prestidigitation.md)

**3/day each:** [comprehend languages](compendium/5e/spells/comprehend-languages.md), [detect magic](compendium/5e/spells/detect-magic.md), [dispel magic](compendium/5e/spells/dispel-magic.md), [identify](compendium/5e/spells/identify.md), [levitate](compendium/5e/spells/levitate.md), [locate object](compendium/5e/spells/locate-object.md), [Tenser's Floating Disk](compendium/5e/spells/tensers-floating-disk.md), [unseen servant](compendium/5e/spells/unseen-servant.md)

**1/day each:** [banishment](compendium/5e/spells/banishment.md), [contact other plane](compendium/5e/spells/contact-other-plane.md), [Drawmij's instant summons](compendium/5e/spells/drawmijs-instant-summons.md), [legend lore](compendium/5e/spells/legend-lore.md), [locate creature](compendium/5e/spells/locate-creature.md), [planar binding](compendium/5e/spells/planar-binding.md), [polymorph](compendium/5e/spells/polymorph.md), [protection from evil and good](compendium/5e/spells/protection-from-evil-and-good.md), [scrying](compendium/5e/spells/scrying.md), [sending](compendium/5e/spells/sending.md), [true seeing](compendium/5e/spells/true-seeing.md)

***Shield (1st-Level Spell; 3/Day).*** When the sage is hit by an attack or targeted by a [magic missile](compendium/5e/spells/magic-missile.md) spell, it calls forth an [invisible](rules/5e/conditions.md#Invisible) barrier of magical force that protects it. Until the start of its next turn, the sage has a +5 bonus to AC, including against the triggering attack, and it takes no damage from magic missile.
