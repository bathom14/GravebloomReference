***Glide.*** If it isn't [incapacitated](rules/5e/conditions.md#Incapacitated) or wearing heavy armor, the hadozee can extend its skin membranes to move up to 5 feet horizontally for every 1 foot it descends in the air.

## Actions

***Dagger.*** *Melee  or Ranged Weapon Attack:* `dice:1d20+4|noform|noparens|text(+4)` to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. *Hit:* `dice:1d4+2|noform|noparens|avg|text(4)` (`1d4 + 2`) piercing damage.

## Reactions

***Safe Descent.*** When it would take damage from a fall, the hadozee extends its skin membranes to reduce the fall's damage to 0, provided it isn't wearing heavy armor.
