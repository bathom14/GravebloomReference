## Traits

***Spellcasting.*** The wood elf is a 10th-level spellcaster. Its spellcasting ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 14, `dice:1d20+6|noform|noparens|text(+6)` to hit with spell attacks). The wood elf has the following wizard spells prepared:

**Cantrips (at will):** [mage hand](compendium/5e/spells/mage-hand.md), [minor illusion](compendium/5e/spells/minor-illusion.md), [poison spray](compendium/5e/spells/poison-spray.md), [ray of frost](compendium/5e/spells/ray-of-frost.md)

**1st level (4 slots):** [mage armor](compendium/5e/spells/mage-armor.md), [magic missile](compendium/5e/spells/magic-missile.md), [shield](compendium/5e/spells/shield.md), [witch bolt](compendium/5e/spells/witch-bolt.md)

**2nd level (3 slots):** [alter self](compendium/5e/spells/alter-self.md), [misty step](compendium/5e/spells/misty-step.md), [web](compendium/5e/spells/web.md)

**3rd level (3 slots):** [fly](compendium/5e/spells/fly.md), [lightning bolt](compendium/5e/spells/lightning-bolt.md)

**4th level (3 slots):** [Evard's black tentacles](compendium/5e/spells/evards-black-tentacles.md), [greater invisibility](compendium/5e/spells/greater-invisibility.md)

**5th level (2 slots):** [cloudkill](compendium/5e/spells/cloudkill.md)

***Innate Spellcasting.*** The wood elf's spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 12). It can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components:

**At will:** [dancing lights](compendium/5e/spells/dancing-lights.md)

**1/day each:** [darkness](compendium/5e/spells/darkness.md), [faerie fire](compendium/5e/spells/faerie-fire.md), [levitate](compendium/5e/spells/levitate.md) (self only)

***Fey Ancestry.*** The wood elf has advantage on saving throws against being [charmed](rules/5e/conditions.md#Charmed), and magic can't put the wood elf to sleep.

## Actions

***Staff.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* `dice:1d20+2|noform|noparens|text(+2)` to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* `dice:1d6-1|noform|noparens|avg|text(2)` (`1d6 - 1`) bludgeoning damage, or `dice:1d8-1|noform|noparens|avg|text(3)` (`1d8 - 1`) bludgeoning damage if used with two hands, plus `dice:1d6|noform|noparens|avg|text(3)` (`d6`) poison damage.

***Summon Demon (1/Day).*** The wood elf magically summons a [quasit](compendium/5e/bestiary/fiend/quasit.md), or attempts to summon a [shadow demon](compendium/5e/bestiary/fiend/shadow-demon.md) with a 50 percent chance of success. The summoned demon appears in an unoccupied space within 60 feet of its summoner, acts as an ally of its summoner, and can't summon other demons. It remains for 10 minutes, until it or its summoner dies, or until its summoner dismisses it as an action.
