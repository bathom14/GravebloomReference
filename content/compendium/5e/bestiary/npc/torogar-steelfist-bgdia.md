## Traits

***Goring Rush.*** Immediately after using the Dash action, Torogar can make one melee attack with his horns.

***Labyrinthine Recall.*** Torogar can perfectly recall any path he has traveled.

***Rage (Recharges after a Short or Long Rest).*** As a bonus action, Torogar can enter a rage that lasts for 1 minute. The rage ends early if Torogar is knocked [unconscious](rules/5e/conditions.md#Unconscious) or if his turn ends and he hasn't attacked a hostile creature or taken damage since his last turn. While raging, Torogar gains the following benefits:

He has advantage on Strength checks and Strength saving throws.

He deals an extra 3 damage when he hits a target with a melee weapon attack.

He has resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage.

***Special Equipment.*** Torogar wears [gauntlets of flaming fury](compendium/5e/items/gauntlets-of-flaming-fury-bgdia.md) and a [belt of fire giant strength](compendium/5e/items/belt-of-fire-giant-strength.md). Without the belt, his Strength is 21. He also carries a [soul coin](compendium/5e/items/soul-coin-bgdia.md).

## Actions

***Multiattack.*** Torogar makes three attacks: two with his scimitars and one with his horns.

***Scimitar.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* `dice:1d20+11|noform|noparens|text(+11)` to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* `dice:2d6+7|noform|noparens|avg|text(14)` (`2d6 + 7`) slashing damage, or `dice:2d6+10|noform|noparens|avg|text(17)` (`2d6 + 10`) slashing damage while raging, plus `dice:1d6|noform|noparens|avg|text(3)` (`d6`) fire damage from the gauntlets of flaming fury.

***Horns.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* `dice:1d20+11|noform|noparens|text(+11)` to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* `dice:2d8+7|noform|noparens|avg|text(16)` (`2d8 + 7`) piercing damage, or `dice:2d8+10|noform|noparens|avg|text(19)` (`2d8 + 10`) piercing damage while raging.
