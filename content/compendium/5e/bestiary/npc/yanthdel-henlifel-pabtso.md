## Traits

***Innate Spellcasting.*** Yanthdel's spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 12). It can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components:

**At will:** [dancing lights](compendium/5e/spells/dancing-lights.md)

**1/day each:** [darkness](compendium/5e/spells/darkness.md), [faerie fire](compendium/5e/spells/faerie-fire.md), [levitate](compendium/5e/spells/levitate.md) (self only)

***Fey Ancestry.*** Yanthdel has advantage on saving throws against being [charmed](rules/5e/conditions.md#Charmed), and magic can't put Yanthdel to sleep.

***Sunlight Sensitivity.*** While in sunlight, Yanthdel has disadvantage on attack rolls, as well as on Wisdom ([Perception](rules/5e/skills.md#Perception)) checks that rely on sight.

## Actions

***Multiattack.*** Yanthdel makes two shortsword attacks.

***Shortsword.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* `dice:1d20+7|noform|noparens|text(+7)` to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* `dice:1d6+4|noform|noparens|avg|text(7)` (`1d6 + 4`) piercing damage plus `dice:3d6|noform|noparens|avg|text(10)` (`3d6`) poison damage.

***Hand Crossbow.*** *Ranged Weapon Attack:* `dice:1d20+7|noform|noparens|text(+7)` to hit, range 30/120 ft., one target. *Hit:* `dice:1d6+4|noform|noparens|avg|text(7)` (`1d6 + 4`) piercing damage, and the target must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or be [poisoned](rules/5e/conditions.md#Poisoned) for 1 hour. If the saving throw fails by 5 or more, the target is also [unconscious](rules/5e/conditions.md#Unconscious) while [poisoned](rules/5e/conditions.md#Poisoned) in this way. The target wakes up if it takes damage or if another creature takes an action to shake it awake.

## Reactions

***Parry.*** Yanthdel adds 3 to its AC against one melee attack that would hit it. To do so, Yanthdel must see the attacker and be wielding a melee weapon.
