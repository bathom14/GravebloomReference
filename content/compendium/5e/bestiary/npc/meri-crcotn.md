## Traits

***Ethereal Sight.*** Meri can see 60 feet into the Ethereal Plane when it is on the Material Plane, and vice versa.

***Incorporeal Movement.*** Meri can move through other creatures and objects as if they were difficult terrain. It takes `dice:1d10|noform|noparens|avg|text(5)` (`d10`) force damage if it ends its turn inside an object.

## Actions

***Withering Touch.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* `dice:1d20+5|noform|noparens|text(+5)` to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* `dice:4d6+3|noform|noparens|avg|text(17)` (`4d6 + 3`) necrotic damage.

***Etherealness.*** Meri enters the Ethereal Plane from the Material Plane, or vice versa. It is visible on the Material Plane while it is in the Border Ethereal, and vice versa, yet it can't affect or be affected by anything on the other plane.

***Horrifying Visage.*** Each non-undead creature within 60 feet of Meri that can see it must succeed on a DC 13 Wisdom saving throw or be [frightened](rules/5e/conditions.md#Frightened) for 1 minute. If the save fails by 5 or more, the target also ages `1d4 × 10` years. A [frightened](rules/5e/conditions.md#Frightened) target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the [frightened](rules/5e/conditions.md#Frightened) condition on itself on a success. If a target's saving throw is successful or the effect ends for it, the target is immune to this ghost's Horrifying Visage for the next 24 hours. The aging effect can be reversed with a  [greater restoration](compendium/5e/spells/greater-restoration.md) spell, but only within 24 hours of it occurring.

***Possession (Recharge 6).*** One humanoid that Meri can see within 5 feet of it must succeed on a DC 13 Charisma saving throw or be possessed by Meri; Meri then disappears, and the target is [incapacitated](rules/5e/conditions.md#Incapacitated) and loses control of its body. Meri now controls the body but doesn't deprive the target of awareness. Meri can't be targeted by any attack, spell, or other effect, except ones that turn undead, and it retains its alignment, Intelligence, Wisdom, Charisma, and immunity to being [charmed](rules/5e/conditions.md#Charmed) and [frightened](rules/5e/conditions.md#Frightened). It otherwise uses the possessed target's statistics, but doesn't gain access to the target's knowledge, class features, or proficiencies.

The possession lasts until the body drops to 0 hit points, Meri ends it as a bonus action, or Meri is turned or forced out by an effect like the [dispel evil and good](compendium/5e/spells/dispel-evil-and-good.md) spell. When the possession ends, Meri reappears in an unoccupied space within 5 feet of the body. The target is immune to this ghost's Possession for 24 hours after succeeding on the saving throw or after the possession ends.
