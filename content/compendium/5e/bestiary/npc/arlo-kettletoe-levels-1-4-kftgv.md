## Actions

***Multiattack.*** Arlo Kettletoe makes three melee attacks: two with its scimitar and one with its dagger. Or Arlo Kettletoe makes two ranged attacks with its daggers.

***Scimitar.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* `dice:1d20+5|noform|noparens|text(+5)` to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* `dice:1d6+3|noform|noparens|avg|text(6)` (`1d6 + 3`) slashing damage.

***Dagger.*** *Melee  or Ranged Weapon Attack:* `dice:1d20+5|noform|noparens|text(+5)` to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. *Hit:* `dice:1d4+3|noform|noparens|avg|text(5)` (`1d4 + 3`) piercing damage.

## Reactions

***Parry.*** Arlo Kettletoe adds 2 to its AC against one melee attack that would hit it. To do so, Arlo Kettletoe must see the attacker and be wielding a melee weapon.
