## Actions

***Multiattack.*** The Keeper makes two battleaxe attacks.

***Battleaxe +1.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* `dice:1d20+10|noform|noparens|text(+10)` to hit, reach 15 ft., one target. *Hit:* `dice:3d8+7|noform|noparens|avg|text(20)` (`3d8 + 7`) slashing damage, or `dice:3d10+7|noform|noparens|avg|text(23)` (`3d10 + 7`) slashing damage if used with both hands.

***Rock.*** *Ranged Weapon Attack:* `dice:1d20+9|noform|noparens|text(+9)` to hit, range 60/240 ft., one target. *Hit:* `dice:4d10+6|noform|noparens|avg|text(28)` (`4d10 + 6`) bludgeoning damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 17 Strength saving throw or be knocked [prone](rules/5e/conditions.md#Prone).

## Reactions

***Rock Catching.*** If a rock or similar object is hurled at the Keeper, the Keeper can, with a successful DC 10 Dexterity saving throw, catch the missile and take no bludgeoning damage from it.
