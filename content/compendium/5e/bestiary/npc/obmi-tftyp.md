***Assassinate.*** During its first turn, Obmi has advantage on attack rolls against any creature that hasn't taken a turn. Any hit Obmi scores against a [surprised](rules/5e/conditions.md#Surprised) creature is a critical hit.

***Evasion.*** If Obmi is subjected to an effect that allows it to make a Dexterity saving throw to take only half damage, Obmi instead takes no damage if it succeeds on the saving throw, and only half damage if it fails.

***Sneak Attack (1/Turn).*** Obmi deals an extra `dice:4d6|noform|noparens|avg|text(14)` (`4d6`) damage when it hits a target with a weapon attack and has advantage on the attack roll, or when the target is within 5 feet of an ally of Obmi that isn't [incapacitated](rules/5e/conditions.md#Incapacitated) and Obmi doesn't have disadvantage on the attack roll.
