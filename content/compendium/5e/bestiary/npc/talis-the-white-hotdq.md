## Traits

***Spellcasting.*** Talis is a 9th-level spellcaster that uses Wisdom as her spellcasting ability (spell save DC 14, `dice:1d20+6|noform|noparens|text(+6)` to hit with spell attacks). Talis has the following spells prepared from the cleric spell list:

**Cantrips (at will):** [guidance](compendium/5e/spells/guidance.md), [resistance](compendium/5e/spells/resistance.md), [thaumaturgy](compendium/5e/spells/thaumaturgy.md)

**1st level (4 slots):** [command](compendium/5e/spells/command.md), [cure wounds](compendium/5e/spells/cure-wounds.md), [healing word](compendium/5e/spells/healing-word.md), [inflict wounds](compendium/5e/spells/inflict-wounds.md)

**2nd level (3 slots):** [blindness/deafness](compendium/5e/spells/blindness-deafness.md), [lesser restoration](compendium/5e/spells/lesser-restoration.md), [spiritual weapon](compendium/5e/spells/spiritual-weapon.md) (spear)

**3rd level (3 slots):** [dispel magic](compendium/5e/spells/dispel-magic.md), [mass healing word](compendium/5e/spells/mass-healing-word.md), [sending](compendium/5e/spells/sending.md)

**4th level (3 slots):** [death ward](compendium/5e/spells/death-ward.md), [freedom of movement](compendium/5e/spells/freedom-of-movement.md)

**5th level (1 slots):** [insect plague](compendium/5e/spells/insect-plague.md)

***Special Equipment.*** Talis has [+1 scale mail](compendium/5e/items/1-armor-dmg.md) and a [wand of winter](compendium/5e/items/wand-of-winter-hotdq.md).

***Fey Ancestry.*** Talis has advantage on saving throws against being [charmed](rules/5e/conditions.md#Charmed), and magic can't put her to sleep.

***Winter Strike (3/Day).*** Once per turn, when Talis hits with a melee attack, she can expend a use of this trait to deal an extra `dice:2d8|noform|noparens|avg|text(9)` (`2d8`) cold damage.
