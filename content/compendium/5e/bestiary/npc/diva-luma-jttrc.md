## Traits

***Assassinate.*** During its first turn, Diva Luma has advantage on attack rolls against any creature that hasn't taken a turn. Any hit Diva Luma scores against a [surprised](rules/5e/conditions.md#Surprised) creature is a critical hit.

***Evasion.*** If Diva Luma is subjected to an effect that allows it to make a Dexterity saving throw to take only half damage, Diva Luma instead takes no damage if it succeeds on the saving throw, and only half damage if it fails.

***Sneak Attack (1/Turn).*** Diva Luma deals an extra `dice:4d6|noform|noparens|avg|text(14)` (`4d6`) damage when it hits a target with a weapon attack and has advantage on the attack roll, or when the target is within 5 feet of an ally of Diva Luma that isn't [incapacitated](rules/5e/conditions.md#Incapacitated) and Diva Luma doesn't have disadvantage on the attack roll.

## Actions

***Multiattack.*** Diva Luma makes two high-heeled shoe attacks.

***High-Heeled Shoe.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* `dice:1d20+6|noform|noparens|text(+6)` to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* `dice:1d6+3|noform|noparens|avg|text(6)` (`1d6 + 3`) piercing damage.
