## Traits

***Rampage.*** When Gash reduces a creature to 0 hit points with a melee attack on its turn, Gash can take a bonus action to move up to half its speed and make a bite attack.

***Wretched.*** Gash has disadvantage on Wisdom checks and Wisdom saving throws because of the physical and mental abuse he has suffered. A [lesser restoration](compendium/5e/spells/lesser-restoration.md) spell rids him of these effects.
