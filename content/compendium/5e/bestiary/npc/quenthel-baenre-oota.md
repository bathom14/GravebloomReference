***Spellcasting.*** Quenthel is a 20th-level spellcaster. Her spellcasting ability is Wisdom (save DC 20, `dice:1d20+12|noform|noparens|text(+12)` to hit with spell attacks). Quenthel has the following cleric spells prepared:

**At will:** Any cleric spell up to 9th level.

**Cantrips (at will):** [guidance](compendium/5e/spells/guidance.md), [poison spray](compendium/5e/spells/poison-spray.md), [resistance](compendium/5e/spells/resistance.md), [spare the dying](compendium/5e/spells/spare-the-dying.md), [thaumaturgy](compendium/5e/spells/thaumaturgy.md)

***Innate Spellcasting.*** Quenthel's spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 19). She can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components:

**At will:** [dancing lights](compendium/5e/spells/dancing-lights.md)

**1/day each:** [darkness](compendium/5e/spells/darkness.md), [faerie fire](compendium/5e/spells/faerie-fire.md), [levitate](compendium/5e/spells/levitate.md) (self only)

***Fey Ancestry.*** Quenthel has advantage on saving throws against being [charmed](rules/5e/conditions.md#Charmed), and magic can't put Quenthel to sleep.

***Sunlight Sensitivity.*** While in sunlight, Quenthel has disadvantage on attack rolls, as well as on Wisdom ([Perception](rules/5e/skills.md#Perception)) checks that rely on sight.

***Special Equipment.*** Quenthel wields a [tentacle rod](compendium/5e/items/tentacle-rod.md).

## Actions

***Multiattack.*** Quenthel makes two scourge attacks.

***Scourge.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* `dice:1d20+5|noform|noparens|text(+5)` to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* `dice:1d6+2|noform|noparens|avg|text(5)` (`1d6 + 2`) piercing damage plus `dice:5d6|noform|noparens|avg|text(17)` (`5d6`) poison damage.

***Summon Demon (1/Day).*** Quenthel attempts to magically summon a [yochlol](compendium/5e/bestiary/fiend/yochlol.md) with a 30 percent chance of success. If the attempt fails, Quenthel takes `dice:1d10|noform|noparens|avg|text(5)` (`d10`) psychic damage. Otherwise, the summoned demon appears in an unoccupied space within 60 feet of its summoner, acts as an ally of its summoner, and can't summon other demons. It remains for 10 minutes, until it or its summoner dies, or until its summoner dismisses it as an action.

***Throne Activation.*** While seated on her throne, Quenthel can use an action on her turn to cast [disintegrate](compendium/5e/spells/disintegrate.md) (save DC 19). A target that fails its saving throw takes `dice:10d6+40|noform|noparens|avg` (`10d6 + 40`) force damage. If this damage reduces the target to 0 hit points, it is disintegrated.
