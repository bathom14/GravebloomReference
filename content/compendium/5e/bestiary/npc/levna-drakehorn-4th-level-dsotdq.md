***Bonus Proficiencies.*** Levna is proficient with simple and martial weapons, shields, and all armor.

***Pack Tactics.*** Levna has advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of her allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally isn't [incapacitated](rules/5e/conditions.md#Incapacitated).

***Second Wind.*** Levna can use a bonus action on her turn to regain hit points equal to `dice:1d10|noform|noparens|avg` (`d10`) + her level. Once she uses this feature, she can't use it again until she finishes a short or long rest.

***Improved Critical.*** Levna's attack rolls now score a critical hit on a roll of 19 or 20 on the `dice:d20|noform|noparens|avg` (`d20`).

***Greatsword.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* `dice:1d20+5|noform|noparens|text(+5)` to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* `dice:2d6+3|noform|noparens|avg|text(10)` (`2d6 + 3`) slashing damage.
