**Cantrips (at will):** [guidance](compendium/5e/spells/guidance.md), [poison spray](compendium/5e/spells/poison-spray.md), [resistance](compendium/5e/spells/resistance.md), [spare the dying](compendium/5e/spells/spare-the-dying.md), [thaumaturgy](compendium/5e/spells/thaumaturgy.md)

**At will:** [dancing lights](compendium/5e/spells/dancing-lights.md)

**1/day each:** [darkness](compendium/5e/spells/darkness.md), [faerie fire](compendium/5e/spells/faerie-fire.md), [levitate](compendium/5e/spells/levitate.md) (self only)

## Actions

***Multiattack.*** Nedylene makes two scourge attacks.

***Scourge +2.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* `dice:1d20+7|noform|noparens|text(+7)` to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* `dice:1d6+4|noform|noparens|avg|text(7)` (`1d6 + 4`) piercing damage plus `dice:5d6|noform|noparens|avg|text(17)` (`5d6`) poison damage.

***Summon Demon (1/Day).*** Nedylene attempts to magically summon a [yochlol](compendium/5e/bestiary/fiend/yochlol.md) with a 30 percent chance of success. If the attempt fails, Nedylene takes `dice:1d10|noform|noparens|avg|text(5)` (`d10`) psychic damage. Otherwise, the summoned demon appears in an unoccupied space within 60 feet of its summoner, acts as an ally of its summoner, and can't summon other demons. It remains for 10 minutes, until it or its summoner dies, or until its summoner dismisses it as an action.
