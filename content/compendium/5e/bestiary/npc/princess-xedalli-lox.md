## Traits

***Fey Ancestry.*** Xedalli has advantage on saving throws it makes to avoid or end the [charmed](rules/5e/conditions.md#Charmed) condition on itself, and magic can't put it to sleep.

***Special Equipment.*** Xedalli wears a suit of [elven chain](compendium/5e/items/elven-chain.md).

***Unusual Nature.*** Xedalli doesn't require sleep.

## Actions

***Multiattack.*** Xedalli makes two Scimitar attacks and uses Radiant Beam (if available).

***Scimitar.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* `dice:1d20+5|noform|noparens|text(+5)` to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* `dice:1d6+2|noform|noparens|avg|text(5)` (`1d6 + 2`) slashing damage plus `dice:3d6|noform|noparens|avg|text(10)` (`3d6`) radiant damage.

***Radiant Beam (3/Day).*** A magical beam of radiance flashes out from Xedalli's hand in a 5-foot-wide, 60-foot-long line. Each creature in the line must make a DC 16 Constitution saving throw, taking `dice:4d8|noform|noparens|avg|text(18)` (`4d8`) radiant damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

***Spellcasting.*** Xedalli casts one of the following spells, using Intelligence as the spellcasting ability:

**1/day each:** [fly](compendium/5e/spells/fly.md), [mislead](compendium/5e/spells/mislead.md), [sending](compendium/5e/spells/sending.md)

## Bonus Actions

***Starlight Step (3/Day).*** Xedalli magically teleports up to 30 feet, along with anything it is wearing or carrying, to an unoccupied space it can see.

***Summon Solar Dragon (1/Day).*** Xedalli has a 50 percent chance of magically summoning a [young solar dragon](compendium/5e/bestiary/dragon/young-solar-dragon-bam.md). A summoned dragon appears in an unoccupied space that the summoner can see, acts on its own initiative count, and is an ally of its summoner. It remains for 10 minutes, until it or its summoner dies, or until its summoner dismisses it as an action.
