# Healing Word
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*1st-level, Abjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Bonus Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

A creature of your choice that you can see within range regains [Hit Points](rules/5e/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) equal to `dice:2d4|noform|noparens|avg` (`2d4`) plus your spellcasting ability modifier.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** The healing increases by `dice:2d4|noform|noparens|avg|text(2d4)` for each spell slot level above 1.

**Classes**: [Cleric](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-cleric.md); [Artificer (Alchemist)](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-alchemist-tce.md "subclass=TCE;class=TCE"); [Bard](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-bard.md); [Druid](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-druid.md); [Sorcerer (Divine Soul)](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-divine-soul-xge.md "subclass=XGE"); [Sorcerer (Divine Soul)](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-divine-soul-xge.md "subclass=XGE;class=XPHB"); [Bard (College of Lore)](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-college-of-lore.md "subclass=XPHB;class=XPHB")
