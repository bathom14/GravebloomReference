# Power Word Heal
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*9th-level, Enchantment*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

A wave of healing energy washes over one creature you can see within range. The target regains all its [Hit Points](rules/5e/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md). If the creature has the [Charmed](rules/5e/conditions.md#Charmed), [Frightened](rules/5e/conditions.md#Frightened), [Paralyzed](rules/5e/conditions.md#Paralyzed), [Poisoned](rules/5e/conditions.md#Poisoned), or [Stunned](rules/5e/conditions.md#Stunned) condition, the condition ends. If the creature has the [Prone](rules/5e/conditions.md#Prone) condition, it can use its [Reaction](rules/5e/variant-rules/reaction-xphb.md) to stand up.

**Classes**: [Cleric](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-cleric.md); [Bard](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-bard.md); [Sorcerer (Divine Soul)](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-divine-soul-xge.md "subclass=XGE"); [Sorcerer (Divine Soul)](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-divine-soul-xge.md "subclass=XGE;class=XPHB")
