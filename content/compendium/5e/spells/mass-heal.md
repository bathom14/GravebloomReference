# Mass Heal
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*9th-level, Abjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

A flood of healing energy flows from you into creatures around you. You restore up to 700 [Hit Points](rules/5e/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md), divided as you choose among any number of creatures that you can see within range. Creatures healed by this spell also have the [Blinded](rules/5e/conditions.md#Blinded), [Deafened](rules/5e/conditions.md#Deafened), and [Poisoned](rules/5e/conditions.md#Poisoned) conditions removed from them.

**Classes**: [Cleric](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-cleric.md); [Bard](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-bard.md); [Sorcerer (Divine Soul)](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-divine-soul-xge.md "subclass=XGE"); [Sorcerer (Divine Soul)](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-divine-soul-xge.md "subclass=XGE;class=XPHB")
