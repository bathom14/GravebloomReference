# Lesser Restoration
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*2nd-level, Abjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Bonus Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You touch a creature and end one condition on it: [Blinded](rules/5e/conditions.md#Blinded), [Deafened](rules/5e/conditions.md#Deafened), [Paralyzed](rules/5e/conditions.md#Paralyzed), or [Poisoned](rules/5e/conditions.md#Poisoned).
