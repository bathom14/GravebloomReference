# Power Word Kill
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*9th-level, Enchantment*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You compel one creature you can see within range to die. If the target has 100 [Hit Points](rules/5e/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) or fewer, it dies. Otherwise, it takes `dice:12d12|noform|noparens|avg` (`12d12`) Psychic damage.
