# Heal
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*6th-level, Abjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Choose a creature that you can see within range. Positive energy washes through the target, restoring 70 [Hit Points](rules/5e/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md). This spell also ends the [Blinded](rules/5e/conditions.md#Blinded), [Deafened](rules/5e/conditions.md#Deafened), and [Poisoned](rules/5e/conditions.md#Poisoned) conditions on the target.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** The healing increases by `dice:10|noform|noparens|avg|text(10)` for each spell slot level above 6.

**Classes**: [Cleric](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-cleric.md); [Bard](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-bard.md); [Druid](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-druid.md); [Sorcerer (Divine Soul)](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-divine-soul-xge.md "subclass=XGE"); [Sorcerer (Divine Soul)](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-divine-soul-xge.md "subclass=XGE;class=XPHB")
