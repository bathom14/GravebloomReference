# Enthrall
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*2nd-level, Enchantment*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 minute

You weave a distracting string of words, causing creatures of your choice that you can see within range to make a Wisdom saving throw. Any creature you or your companions are fighting automatically succeeds on this save. On a failed save, a target has a -10 penalty to Wisdom ([Perception](rules/5e/skills.md#Perception)) checks and Passive [Perception](rules/5e/skills.md#Perception) until the spell ends.

**Classes**: [Bard](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-bard.md); [Warlock](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-warlock.md)
