# Shining Smite
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*2nd-level, Transmutation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Bonus Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

The target hit by the strike takes an extra `dice:2d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`2d6`) Radiant damage from the attack. Until the spell ends, the target sheds [Bright Light](rules/5e/variant-rules/bright-light-xphb.md) in a 5-foot radius, attack rolls against it have [Advantage](rules/5e/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md), and it can't benefit from the [Invisible](rules/5e/conditions.md#Invisible) condition.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** The damage increases by `dice:1d6|noform|noparens|avg|text(d6)` for each spell slot level above 2.
