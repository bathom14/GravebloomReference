# Color Spray
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*1st-level, Illusion*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self (15-foot Cone)
- **Components:** V, S, M (a pinch of colorful sand)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You launch a dazzling array of flashing, colorful light. Each creature in a 15-foot [Cone](rules/5e/variant-rules/cone-area-of-effect-xphb.md) originating from you must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or have the [Blinded](rules/5e/conditions.md#Blinded) condition until the end of your next turn.
