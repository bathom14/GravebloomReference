# Fount of Moonlight
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*4th-level, Evocation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes

A cool light wreathes your body for the duration, emitting [Bright Light](rules/5e/variant-rules/bright-light-xphb.md) in a 20-foot radius and [Dim Light](rules/5e/variant-rules/dim-light-xphb.md) for an additional 20 feet.

Until the spell ends, you have [Resistance](rules/5e/variant-rules/resistance-xphb.md) to Radiant damage, and your melee attacks deal an extra `dice:2d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`2d6`) Radiant damage on a hit.

In addition, immediately after you take damage from a creature you can see within 60 feet of yourself, you can take a [Reaction](rules/5e/variant-rules/reaction-xphb.md) to force the creature to make a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, the creature has the [Blinded](rules/5e/conditions.md#Blinded) condition until the end of your next turn.
