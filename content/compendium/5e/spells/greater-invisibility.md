# Greater Invisibility
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*4th-level, Illusion*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

A creature you touch has the [Invisible](rules/5e/conditions.md#Invisible) condition until the spell ends.
