# Foresight
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*9th-level, Divination*  

- **Casting time:** 1 minute
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (a hummingbird feather)
- **Duration:** 8 hours

You touch a willing creature and bestow a limited ability to see into the immediate future. For the duration, the target has [Advantage](rules/5e/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on [D20 Tests](rules/5e/variant-rules/d20-test-xphb.md), and other creatures have [Disadvantage](rules/5e/variant-rules/disadvantage-xphb.md) on attack rolls against it. The spell ends early if you cast it again.

**Classes**: [Bard](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-bard.md); [Druid](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-druid.md); [Warlock](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-warlock.md); [Wizard](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-wizard.md); [Cleric (Arcana Domain)](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-arcana-domain-scag.md "subclass=SCAG;class=XPHB"); [Cleric (Arcana Domain)](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-arcana-domain-scag.md "subclass=SCAG"); [Wizard (Diviner)](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-diviner.md "subclass=XPHB;class=XPHB")
