# Gravity Fissure
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*6th-level, Evocation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self (100-foot Line)
- **Components:** V, S, M (a fistful of iron filings)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You manifest a ravine of gravitational energy in a line originating from you that is 100 feet long and 5 feet wide. Each creature in that line must make a Constitution saving throw, taking `dice:8d8|noform|noparens|avg` (`8d8`) force damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

Each creature within 10 feet of the line but not in it must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take `dice:8d8|noform|noparens|avg` (`8d8`) force damage and be pulled toward the line until the creature is in its area.

**At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, the damage increases by `dice:1d8|noform|noparens|avg|text(d8)` for each slot level above 6th.
