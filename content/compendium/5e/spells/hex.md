# Hex
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*1st-level, Enchantment*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Bonus Action
- **Range:** 90 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (the petrified eye of a newt)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour

You place a curse on a creature that you can see within range. Until the spell ends, you deal an extra `dice:1d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`d6`) Necrotic damage to the target whenever you hit it with an attack roll. Also, choose one ability when you cast the spell. The target has [Disadvantage](rules/5e/variant-rules/disadvantage-xphb.md) on ability checks made with the chosen ability.

If the target drops to 0 [Hit Points](rules/5e/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) before this spell ends, you can take a [Bonus Action](rules/5e/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) on a later turn to curse a new creature.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** Your [Concentration](rules/5e/conditions.md#Concentration) can last longer with a spell slot of level 2 (up to 4 hours), 3-4 (up to 8 hours), or 5+ (24 hours).

**Classes**: [Bard](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-bard.md); [Warlock (Great Old One Patron)](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-great-old-one-patron.md "subclass=XPHB;class=XPHB"); [Warlock](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-warlock.md)
