# Sapping Sting
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*cantrip, Necromancy*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You sap the vitality of one creature you can see in range. The target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take `dice:1d4|noform|noparens|avg` (`d4`) necrotic damage and fall [prone](rules/5e/conditions.md#Prone).

This spell's damage increases by `dice:1d4|noform|noparens|avg` (`d4`) when you reach 5th level (`dice:2d4|noform|noparens|avg` (`2d4`)), 11th level (`dice:3d4|noform|noparens|avg` (`3d4`)), and 17th level (`dice:4d4|noform|noparens|avg` (`4d4`)).
