# Befuddlement
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*8th-level, Enchantment*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 150 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a key ring with no keys)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You blast the mind of a creature that you can see within range. The target makes an Intelligence saving throw.

On a failed save, the target takes `dice:10d12|noform|noparens|avg` (`10d12`) Psychic damage and can't cast spells or take the [Magic](rules/5e/actions.md#Magic) action. At the end of every 30 days, the target repeats the save, ending the effect on a success. The effect can also be ended by the [Greater Restoration](compendium/5e/spells/greater-restoration.md), [Heal](compendium/5e/spells/heal.md), or [Wish](compendium/5e/spells/wish.md) spell.

On a successful save, the target takes half as much damage only.

**Classes**: [Bard](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-bard.md); [Druid](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-druid.md); [Warlock](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-warlock.md); [Wizard](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-wizard.md); [Cleric (Arcana Domain)](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-arcana-domain-scag.md "subclass=SCAG;class=XPHB"); [Cleric (Arcana Domain)](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-arcana-domain-scag.md "subclass=SCAG")
