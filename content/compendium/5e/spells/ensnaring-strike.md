# Ensnaring Strike
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*1st-level, Conjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Bonus Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

As you hit the target, grasping vines appear on it, and it makes a Strength saving throw. A Large or larger creature has [Advantage](rules/5e/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on this save. On a failed save, the target has the [Restrained](rules/5e/conditions.md#Restrained) condition until the spell ends. On a successful save, the vines shrivel away, and the spell ends.

While [Restrained](rules/5e/conditions.md#Restrained), the target takes `dice:1d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`d6`) Piercing damage at the start of each of its turns. The target or a creature within reach of it can take an action to make a Strength ([Athletics](rules/5e/skills.md#Athletics)) check against your spell save DC. On a success, the spell ends.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** The damage increases by `dice:1d6|noform|noparens|avg|text(d6)` for each spell slot level above 1.

**Classes**: [Ranger](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-ranger.md); [Bard](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-bard.md); [Paladin (Oath of the Ancients)](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-oath-of-the-ancients.md "subclass=XPHB;class=XPHB"); [Paladin (Oath of the Ancients)](compendium/5e/lists/list-spells-classes-oath-of-the-ancients-phb.md)
