# Burning
*Generic Hazard*  

A burning creature or object takes `dice:1d4|noform|noparens|avg` (`d4`) Fire damage at the start of each of its turns. As an action, you can extinguish fire on yourself by giving yourself the [Prone](rules/5e/conditions.md#Prone) condition and rolling on the ground. The fire also goes out if it is doused, submerged, or suffocated.

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 362*
