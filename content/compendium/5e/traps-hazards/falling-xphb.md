# Falling
*Generic Hazard*  

A creature that falls takes `dice:1d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`d6`) Bludgeoning damage at the end of the fall for every 10 feet it fell, to a maximum of `dice:20d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`20d6`). When the creature lands, it has the [Prone](rules/5e/conditions.md#Prone) condition unless it avoids taking any damage from the fall.

A creature that falls into water or another liquid can use its [Reaction](rules/5e/variant-rules/reaction-xphb.md) to make a DC 15 Strength ([Athletics](rules/5e/skills.md#Athletics)) or Dexterity ([Acrobatics](rules/5e/skills.md#Acrobatics)) check to hit the surface head or feet first. On a successful check, any damage resulting from the fall is halved.

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 367*
