# Heavy Precipitation
*Environmental Hazard*  

Everything within an area of heavy rain or heavy snowfall is [Lightly Obscured](rules/5e/variant-rules/lightly-obscured-xphb.md), and creatures in the area have [Disadvantage](rules/5e/variant-rules/disadvantage-xphb.md) on all Wisdom ([Perception](rules/5e/skills.md#Perception)) checks. Heavy rain also extinguishes open flames.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 69*
