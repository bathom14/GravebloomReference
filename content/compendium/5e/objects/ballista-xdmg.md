# Ballista
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 96*  

A Ballista is a massive crossbow that fires heavy bolts. Loading a Ballista requires the [Utilize](rules/5e/actions.md#Utilize) action, and aiming it requires another [Utilize](rules/5e/actions.md#Utilize) action. Then a crew member can take the Ballista Bolt action.
