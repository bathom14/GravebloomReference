# Kenku
*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 282*  

- **Ability Scores**: dex +2
- **Size**: Medium
- **Speed**: 30 ft.

## Traits

### Ambusher

In the first round of a combat, you have advantage on attack rolls against any creature who is [surprised](rules/5e/conditions.md#Surprised).

### Mimicry

You can mimic any sounds you have heard, including voices. A creature that hears the sounds can tell they are imitations with a successful DC 14 Wisdom ([Insight](rules/5e/skills.md#Insight)) check.

### Languages

You can read and write Common and Auran, but you can only speak using your Mimicry trait.
