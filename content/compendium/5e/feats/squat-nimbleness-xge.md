# Squat Nimbleness
*Source: Xanathar's Guide to Everything p. 75*  

**Prerequisite**: dwarf or a Small race

You are uncommonly nimble for your race. You gain the following benefits:

- Increase your Strength or Dexterity by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
- Increase your walking speed by 5 feet.  
- You gain proficiency in the [Acrobatics](rules/5e/skills.md#Acrobatics) or [Athletics](rules/5e/skills.md#Athletics) skill (your choice).  
- You have advantage on any Strength ([Athletics](rules/5e/skills.md#Athletics)) or Dexterity ([Acrobatics](rules/5e/skills.md#Acrobatics)) check you make to escape from being [grappled](rules/5e/conditions.md#Grappled).
