# Righteous Heritor
*Source: Sigil and the Outlands p. 11*  

**Prerequisite**: 4th

You can channel cosmic forces of good to gain these benefits:

- **Ability Score Increase.** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
- **Soothe Pain.** When you or a creature within 30 feet of you takes damage, you can use your reaction to reduce that damage by `dice:1d10|noform|noparens|avg` (`d10`) + your proficiency bonus. You can use this benefit a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, and you regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest.
