# Flash Recall
*Source: Tal'Dorei Campaign Setting Reborn p. 190*  

**Prerequisite**: Spellcasting feature from a class that prepares spells

You've developed the ability to instantly recall an unprepared spell in moments of sudden necessity.

As a bonus action, you prepare a spell of 1st level or higher from your spellbook (if you're a [wizard](compendium/5e/classes/wizard-phb.md)) or from your class spell list (if you're not a [wizard](compendium/5e/classes/wizard-phb.md)). This spell must be of a level for which you have spell slots, and it replaces another spell of an equal or higher level that you had previously prepared.

Once you use this feat to recall a spell, you can't do so again until you complete a short or long rest.

> [!note] Rules Tip: Prepared Spells
> 
> The Flash Recall feat requires you to be a spellcaster who prepares spells. This includes classes such as [clerics](compendium/5e/classes/cleric-phb.md), [druids](compendium/5e/classes/druid-phb.md), and [paladins](compendium/5e/classes/paladin-phb.md), who prepare spells each day from their class list, and [wizards](compendium/5e/classes/wizard-phb.md), who prepare spells from a spellbook. Classes such as [bards](compendium/5e/classes/bard-phb.md) and [sorcerers](compendium/5e/classes/sorcerer-phb.md), who inherently know their spells rather than preparing them, can't benefit from this feat.
