# Skilled
*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 201. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*  

You gain proficiency in any combination of three [skills](compendium/5e/tables/skill-list-skills-xphb.md) or tools of your choice.

**Repeatable.** You can take this feat more than once.
