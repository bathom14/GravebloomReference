# Pact of the Chain
*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 157. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span>*  

You learn the [Find Familiar](compendium/5e/spells/find-familiar.md) spell and can cast it as a [Magic](rules/5e/actions.md#Magic) action without expending a spell slot.

When you cast the spell, you choose one of the normal forms for your familiar or one of the following special forms: [Imp](compendium/5e/bestiary/fiend/imp.md), [Pseudodragon](compendium/5e/bestiary/dragon/pseudodragon.md), [Quasit](compendium/5e/bestiary/fiend/quasit.md), [Skeleton](compendium/5e/bestiary/undead/skeleton.md), [Slaad Tadpole](compendium/5e/bestiary/aberration/slaad-tadpole.md), [Sphinx of Wonder](compendium/5e/bestiary/celestial/sphinx-of-wonder.md), [Sprite](compendium/5e/bestiary/fey/sprite.md), or [Venomous Snake](compendium/5e/bestiary/beast/venomous-snake.md) (see appendix B for the familiar's stat block).

Additionally, when you take the [Attack](rules/5e/actions.md#Attack) action, you can forgo one of your own attacks to allow your familiar to make one attack of its own with its [Reaction](rules/5e/variant-rules/reaction-xphb.md).
