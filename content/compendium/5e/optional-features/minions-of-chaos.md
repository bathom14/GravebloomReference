*Source: Player's Handbook p. 111. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.1)'>SRD</span>*

You can cast [conjure elemental](compendium/5e/spells/conjure-elemental.md) once using a warlock spell slot. You can't do so again until you finish a long rest.
