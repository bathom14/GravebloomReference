# Helm of Awareness
*Source: Tasha's Cauldron of Everything p. 21*  

**Prerequisite**: Level 10

While wearing this helmet, a creature has advantage on initiative rolls. In addition, the wearer can't be [surprised](rules/5e/conditions.md#Surprised), provided it isn't [incapacitated](rules/5e/conditions.md#Incapacitated).
