# Grasp of Hadar
*Source: Xanathar's Guide to Everything p. 57*  

**Prerequisite**: [eldritch blast](compendium/5e/spells/eldritch-blast.md) cantrip

Once on each of your turns when you hit a creature with your [eldritch blast](compendium/5e/spells/eldritch-blast.md), you can move that creature in a straight line 10 feet closer to you.
