# Lance of Lethargy
*Source: Xanathar's Guide to Everything p. 57*  

**Prerequisite**: [eldritch blast](compendium/5e/spells/eldritch-blast.md) cantrip

Once on each of your turns when you hit a creature with your [eldritch blast](compendium/5e/spells/eldritch-blast.md), you can reduce that creature's speed by 10 feet until the end of your next turn.
