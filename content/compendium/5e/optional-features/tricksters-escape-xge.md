# Trickster's Escape
*Source: Xanathar's Guide to Everything p. 57*  

**Prerequisite**: Level 7

You can cast [freedom of movement](compendium/5e/spells/freedom-of-movement.md) once on yourself without expending a spell slot. You regain the ability to do so when you finish a long rest.
