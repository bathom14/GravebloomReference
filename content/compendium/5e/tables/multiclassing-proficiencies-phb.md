# Multiclassing Proficiencies
*Source: Player's Handbook p. 164. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.1)'>SRD</span>* 

**Multiclassing Proficiencies**

| Class | Proficiencies Gained |
|-------|----------------------|
| [Barbarian](compendium/5e/classes/barbarian-phb.md) | Shields, simple weapons, martial weapons |
| [Bard](compendium/5e/classes/bard-phb.md) | Light armor, one skill of your choice, one musical instrument of your choice |
| [Cleric](compendium/5e/classes/cleric-phb.md) | Light armor, medium armor, shields |
| [Druid](compendium/5e/classes/druid-phb.md) | Light armor, medium armor, shields (druids will not wear armor or use shields made of metal) |
| [Fighter](compendium/5e/classes/fighter-phb.md) | Light armor, medium armor, shields, simple weapons, martial weapons |
| [Monk](compendium/5e/classes/monk-phb.md) | Simple weapons, shortswords |
| [Paladin](compendium/5e/classes/paladin-phb.md) | Light armor, medium armor, shields, simple weapons, martial weapons |
| [Ranger](compendium/5e/classes/ranger-phb.md) | Light armor, medium armor, shields, simple weapons, martial weapons, one skill from the class's skill list |
| [Rogue](compendium/5e/classes/rogue-phb.md) | Light armor, one skill from the class's skill list, [thieves' tools](compendium/5e/items/thieves-tools.md) |
| [Sorcerer](compendium/5e/classes/sorcerer-phb.md) | — |
| [Warlock](compendium/5e/classes/warlock-phb.md) | Light armor, simple weapons |
| [Wizard](compendium/5e/classes/wizard-phb.md) | — |
