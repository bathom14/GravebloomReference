Following the tracks for `dice:1d4\|noform\|noparens\|avg` (`d4`) hours leads to an encounter with [gnolls](compendium/5e/bestiary/fiend/gnoll-warrior.md), or the discovery of dead [gnolls](compendium/5e/bestiary/fiend/gnoll-warrior.md) with elven arrows sticking out of their flea-ridden corpses. |

If the result indicates a monster encounter, the indicated monster or monsters arrive in `dice:1d4\|noform\|noparens\|avg` (`d4`) minutes. New arrivals other than [gnolls](compendium/5e/bestiary/fiend/gnoll-warrior.md), [hyenas](compendium/5e/bestiary/beast/hyena.md), [owlbears](compendium/5e/bestiary/monstrosity/owlbear.md), and [displacer beasts](compendium/5e/bestiary/monstrosity/displacer-beast.md) are friendly toward the [scouts](compendium/5e/bestiary/humanoid/scout.md). |
| 11 | `dice:2d4\|noform\|noparens\|avg` (`2d4`) [pixies](compendium/5e/bestiary/fey/pixie.md) (50%) or `dice:2d4\|noform\|noparens\|avg` (`2d4`) [sprites](compendium/5e/bestiary/fey/sprite.md) (50%) |

Berries regrow at midnight, but if all its berries are picked, the plant becomes nonmagical and grows no more berries. |
