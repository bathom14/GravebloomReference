Your understanding of psionic energy has allowed you to selectively restructure its composition within yourself, enabling you to tesselate quanta of psionic force instead of maintaining more stable arrangements.

Whenever your dΨ would shrink or grow, you may choose to tesselate it instead of shrinking or growing normally. Based on the λ dΨ that you are shrinking or growing into, your dΨ is now replaced with a number of d4 equal to the λ dΨ. For example, at λ dΨ = 2 (where normally dΨ = d6), your tessellated dΨ would be 2d4. At λ dΨ = 5 (where normally dΨ = d6), your tessellated dΨ would be 5d4. Each d4 rolled for a tessellated dΨ applies grow or shrink triggers, and any effect that would normally allow for modulation of dΨ results still functions, but only applies to a single d4 rolled. When your tessellated dΨ would shrink or grow (as a result of the number of growth triggers exceeding shrink triggers, or vice-versa), you must return to a non-tessellated dΨ of the appropriate λ dΨ value. Each resolved growth or shrink result for a tessellated dΨ only counts as a single growth or shrink.
