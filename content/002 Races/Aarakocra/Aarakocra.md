# Aarakocra

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Dexterity score increases by 2, and one other ability score of your choice increases by 1. Your Constitution score decreases by 1.

**Age.** Aarakocra tend to live lifespans similar to humans, reaching maturing around 16 and rarely living past 80.

**Alignment** Aarakokra tend towards Neutral alignments

**Creature Type.** You have colorful plumage, and appear similar to a humanoid parrot. You are a Humanoid.

**Size.** Your size is Medium. You are between 4 ½ and 5 ½ feet tall and weigh between 80 and 120 pounds.

**Speed.** Your walking speed is 30 feet.

**Flight.** You have a flying speed equal to your walking speed. If you are wearing medium armor, this flying speed is halved. You can’t use this flying speed if you’re wearing heavy armor.

**Talons.** You have talons that you can use to make unarmed strikes. When you hit with them, the strike deals 1d6 + your Strength modifier slashing damage, instead of the bludgeoning damage normal for an unarmed strike.

**Wind Caller.** Starting at 3rd level, you can cast the [Gust of Wind](http://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:gust-of-wind) spell with this trait, without requiring a material component. You and can cast it without expending a spell slot a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus. You regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest. You can also cast the spell using any spell slots you have of 2nd level or higher.
* Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma is your spellcasting ability for it when you cast Gust of Wind with this trait (choose when you select this race).

**Mimicry.** You can accurately mimic sounds you have heard, including voices. A creature that hears the sounds you make can tell they are imitations with a successful Wisdom (Insight) check opposed by your Charisma (Deception) check.

**Languages.** Your character can speak, read, and write Aarakocran and one other language that you and your DM agree is appropriate for the character.
