# Hadozee

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Dexterity increases by 2, and your Charisma increases by 1

**Age.** Hadozee typically live to around 70 years of age

**Alignment.** The ideals of the Hadozee vary as much as their borders.

**Creature Type.** You are a Humanoid.

**Size.** You are Medium or Small. You choose the size when you select this race.

**Speed.** Your walking speed is 30 feet, and you have a climbing speed equal to your walking speed.

**Dexterous Feet.** As a bonus action, you can use your feet to manipulate an object, open or close a door or container, or pick up or set down a Tiny object. You may also be able to perform simple tasks, at your DM’s discretion. You may Use an Object this way during your turn.

**Glide.** If you are not incapacitated or wearing heavy armor, you can extend your skin membranes and glide. When you do so, you can perform the following aerial maneuvers.

* When you fall at least 5 feet, you can move up to 5 feet horizontally for every 1 foot you descend.

* When you would take damage from a fall, you can use your reaction to reduce the fall’s damage to 0.

**Hadozee Dodge.** The magic that runs in your veins heightens your natural defenses. When you take damage, you can use your reaction to roll a d6. Add your proficiency bonus to the number rolled, and reduce the damage you take by an amount equal to that total (minimum of 0 damage). You can use this trait a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus. You regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest.

**Inclination Compass.** All Hadozee possess an innate awareness of their position relative to the Red Rock. You can utilize this sense in a manner similar to a traditional compass. Some effects may disrupt this trait. You also have proficiency with your choice of either Cartographer's or Navigator's tools.

**Monkey Build.** You are proficient in the Acrobatics and Sleight of Hand skills.

**Languages.** You can speak, read, and write Hadozee, Dwarvish and one other language that you and your DM agree is appropriate.
