An Arcane Archer studies a unique elven method of archery that weaves magic into attacks to produce supernatural effects.

### Arcane Archer Lore

At 3rd level, you learn magical theory or some of the secrets of nature.

### Arcane Shot

At 3rd level, you learn to unleash special magical effects with some of your shots. You learn two Arcane Shot options of your choice, and you gain additional options as you level.

### Magic Arrow

At 7th level, your magic infuses your arrows with potency.

### Curving Shot

At 7th level, you learn to direct an errant arrow toward a new target.

### Ever-Ready Shot

Starting at 15th level, your magical archery is ever ready.
