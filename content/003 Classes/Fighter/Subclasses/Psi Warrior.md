Awakened to the psionic power within, a Psi Warrior augments their attacks and defenses with telekinetic force.

### Psionic Power

At 3rd level, you harbor a wellspring of psionic energy represented by Psionic Energy dice.

### Telekinetic Adept

At 7th level, you have mastered new ways to use your telekinetic abilities.

### Guarded Mind

At 10th level, your mind is a well of calm resilience.

### Bulwark of Force

At 15th level, you can shield those around you with telekinetic force.

### Telekinetic Master

At 18th level, your ability to move creatures and objects with your mind is unmatched.
