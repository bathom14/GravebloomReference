The Cavalier excels at mounted combat and protecting allies.

### Bonus Proficiency

When you choose this archetype at 3rd level, you gain proficiency in one of the following skills of your choice: Animal Handling, History, Insight, Performance, or Persuasion.

### Born to the Saddle

Starting at 3rd level, your mastery as a rider becomes apparent.

### Unwavering Mark

At 3rd level, you can menace your foes, foiling their attacks and punishing them for harming others.

### Warding Maneuver

At 7th level, you can fend off strikes directed at you, your mount, or other creatures nearby.

### Hold the Line

At 10th level, you can hinder the movement of a creature by striking it.

### Ferocious Charger

Starting at 15th level, you can run down your foes.

### Vigilant Defender

At 18th level, you respond to danger with extraordinary vigilance.
