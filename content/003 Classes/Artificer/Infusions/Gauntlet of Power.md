---
title: Gauntlet of Power
note_type: infusion
class_req: Artificer
level_req: 5
---
Prerequisite: 5th-level Artificer
Item: gauntlet (requires attunement)

The wielder can use the gauntlet to make melee spell attacks using your Intelligence. On a hit, it deals 1d8 force damage + your Intelligence modifier. At 11th level the damage becomes 2d8, and at 17th level it becomes 3d8.
