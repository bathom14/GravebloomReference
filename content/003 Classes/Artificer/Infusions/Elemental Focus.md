---
title: Elemental Focus
note_type: infusion
class_req: Artificer
---
Item: arcane focus (requires attunement)

When the wielder casts a spell that deals damage, they can change the spell’s damage type to acid, cold, fire, lightning, poison, or thunder.
At 17th level, this focus grants a +1 bonus to spell attack rolls and to the wielder’s Spell save DC.
