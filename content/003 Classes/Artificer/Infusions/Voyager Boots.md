---
title: Voyager Boots
note_type: infusion
class_req: Artificer
level_req: 11
---
Prerequisite: 11th-level Artificer
Item: pair of boots (requires attunement)

As a bonus action, the wearer can teleport up to 15 feet to an unoccupied space they can see. At 17th level, this distance increases to 30 feet.
