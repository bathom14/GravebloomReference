---
title: Repulsion Gauntlets
note_type: infusion
class_req: Artificer
level_req: 5
---
Prerequisite: 5th-level Artificer
Item: pair of gloves or gauntlets (requires attunement)

When the wielder hits a creature with an unarmed strike or gauntlet attack, they can expend one Charge to force a Strength save (Large or larger creatures have advantage). On a failure, the target is pushed 15 feet in a straight line.
This item has Charges equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum 1), regaining all expended Charges at dawn.
At 11th level, the gauntlets gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls (increasing to +2 at 17th level).
