---
title: Guardian Gauntlets
note_type: infusion
class_req: Artificer
level_req: 17
---
Prerequisite: 17th-level Artificer
Item: pair of gauntlets (requires attunement)

When a creature ends its turn within 30 feet of the wearer, the wearer can expend one Charge as a reaction to force it to make a Strength save (Huge or larger has advantage). On a failure, it is pulled up to 30 feet toward the wearer to an unoccupied space of its choice. If pulled within 5 feet, the wearer can make one melee weapon attack against it.
This item has Charges equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum 1), regaining all expended Charges at dawn.
