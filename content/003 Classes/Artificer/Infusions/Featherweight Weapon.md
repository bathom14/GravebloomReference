---
title: Featherweight Weapon
note_type: infusion
class_req: Artificer
---
Item: simple or martial melee weapon (requires attunement)

If the weapon has the heavy and/or two‑handed properties, it loses them. If it has neither heavy nor two‑handed, it gains the light property.
At 11th level, the weapon gains a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls. This bonus increases to +2 at 17th level.
