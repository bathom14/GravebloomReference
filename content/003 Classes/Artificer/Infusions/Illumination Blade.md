title: Illumination Blade
note_type: infusion
class_req: Artificer
level_req: 5
---
Prerequisite: 5th-level Artificer
Item: hilt-sized Tiny object (requires attunement)

As a bonus action, the wielder can cause a blade of radiant light to spring forth. The item emits bright light in a 15‑foot radius and dim light for 15 feet beyond. It is a simple melee weapon with finesse that deals 1d8 radiant damage on a hit.
At 11th level, the weapon gains a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls (increasing to +2 at 17th level).
