---
title: Enhanced Instrument
note_type: infusion
class_req: Artificer
---
Item: musical instrument (requires attunement)

The wielder is proficient with this instrument and can use it as a spellcasting focus. When making an ability check using the instrument, add your Intelligence modifier (minimum +1).
At 11th level, the instrument grants a +1 bonus to the wielder’s Spell save DC when used as a focus (increasing to +2 at 17th level).
