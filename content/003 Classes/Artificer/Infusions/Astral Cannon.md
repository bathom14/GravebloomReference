---
title: Astral Cannon
note_type: infusion
class_req: Artificer
level_req: 17
---
Prerequisite: 17th-level Artificer
Item: gauntlet, arcane focus, or metal rod

In place of an attack, the wielder makes a ranged spell attack against a target within 60 feet; on a hit, the target takes 2d10 force damage.
As an action, the wielder can expend one Charge to fire two dimensional motes at two unoccupied flat surfaces within 60 feet, creating a 5‑by‑5‑foot portal connecting them. The portal lasts 1 minute, closing early if five creatures pass through it or if this infusion creates another portal.
This item has Charges equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum 1), regaining all expended Charges at dawn.
