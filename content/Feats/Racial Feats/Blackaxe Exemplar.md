**Prerequisites:** 4th level, [[Giff]]

You are beginning to master the Way of the Blackaxe. You gain the following benefits.

* **Earned Honor.** The next time you reach a port, you receive a Lesser Blackaxe from the Ikons of Jakkar. The Lesser Blackaxe can never be replaced if it is lost or destroyed. You will be shunned by the Ikons until you recover a Lesser Blackaxe. It is taboo to modify a Blackaxe, but is not expressly forbidden. 

* **Throw Down.** Whenever you knock a creature prone or land a critical hit, you regain one usage of the Blessing of Jakkar feature. 

* **Low Hanging Fruit.** Whenever you land a melee attack, you may make an additional attack with a loaded Blackaxe you are holding.

* **Aggressive March.** Your Headfirst Charge feature now requires targets to succeed a DC equal to 10 + your proficiency bonus + your Strength modifier instead. If they fail the save, they now take bludgeoning damage equal to 2d8 + your Strength modifier. The base damage die increases by 1d8 when you reach 11th level (3d8), and 2d8 (4d8) whe you reach 17th level.

***Double-Barrel Action.*** :: The Lesser Blackaxe is a two-handed break-action martial firearm that deals 2d8 piercing damage and uses shells for ammunition. The Lesser Blackaxe has two barrels, giving it a magazine of two. It may be fired twice before reloading. As an action, you may reload two empty barrels. As a bonus action, you may relead one empty barrel. It has a range of 100/200.

***Hippo Sized.*** The Lesser Blackaxe is extremely bulky. It requires a Strength score of 21 to use without disadvantage for most creatures, but the hippo-like build of the Giff allows them to wield it with a Strength score of 15. It uses the Strength modifier for attack and damage rolls. 

***Blackaxe.*** :: The Lesser Blackaxe is considered a magical weapon and requires attunement. You may may add + 1 to all attack and damage rolls made with it.

***Gnomish Handiwork*** :: The Lesser Blackaxe features an advanced recoil dampening stock, granting it +1 to attack rolls for every consecutive attack made on the same turn. The Lesser Blackaxe can never become jammed.
