**Prerequisites:** 4th level, [[Forest Gnome]]

Your skills in working with mushrooms are exceptional, even among others of your race. You gain the following benefits:

* **Facilitated Growth.** Increase your Wisdom or Intelligence ability score by 1. You can cast the Plant Growth spell once per long rest. Wisdom or Intelligence (based on stat increase) is your spellcasting ability for this spell.

* **Prolific Forager.** You may forage an additional time for each long rest. When you cast Plant Growth for its 8 hour duration, you may add half of your proficiency bonus, rounded down, to any forage actions you take in the affected area for the next 24 hours.

* **Tincture Master.** Increase your Wisdom or Intelligence ability score by 1, You have proficiency with artisan’s tools (alchemist’s supplies). Using those tools, you can spend 1 hour to extract the effects of multiple mushrooms and infuse them into a single tincture, destroying the mushrooms in the process. The number of mushrooms you can infuse into a single tincture is determined by your proficiency bonus. The resulting tincture has all the effects of the mushrooms destroyed in its production. The effects are applied in the order of the highest mushroom value effect to the lowest.
   - As an action, a creature can consume the tincture or administer it to an incapacitated creature. The effects of the tincture are considered magic. Its weight is 0.2 lbs. The effects of the tincture apply sequentially in descending order of the roll value of the mushroom which contributed the effect. The effects of the tincture do not apply to Constructs or Undead.
