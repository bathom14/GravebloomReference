**Prerequisites:** [[Mountain Dwarf]], [[Hobgoblin]], [[Half-Orc]], [[Giff]], [[Orc]]

You become much better at displaying your martial power on the battlefield. You gain the following benefits.

* **Armory.** You gain proficiency with one martial weapon of your choice.

* **Weak Point.** When attacking with a martial weapon, your critical strength range increases by one.

* **Press Advantage.** Whenever you have advantage on an attack roll using a martial weapon, you can reroll one of the dice once.
