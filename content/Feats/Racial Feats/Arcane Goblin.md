**Prerequisites:** 4th level, [[Goblin]]

Rare among your people, you have awoken with the gift. You gain the following benefits.

* **So Anyways.** You know the Eldritch Blast cantrip. Charisma is your spellcasting ability for this spell.

* **Tantalizing Stench.** You learn the Grasp of Hadar and Cloak of Flies Eldritch Invocations.
