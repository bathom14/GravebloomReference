**Prerequisites:** [[Desert Dwarf]], [[Half-Orc]], [[Hadozee]], [[Human]]

You are a gifted sailor among your people. You recieve the following benefits.

* **Deft Maneuvers.** Your Dexterity score increases by 1.

* **Personal Vessel.** The next time you reach a port, you recieve personal vessel at no cost. It is yours, but has no special protection. It can be damaged or destroyed, and those consequences are permanent. It takes an appearance that you and your DM agree are appropriate.

* **Competent Navigator.** You gain proficiency with your choice of Cartographer's Tools or Navigator's Tools.
