**Prerequisites:** [[Goblin]]

Goblins grow rapidly until the day they die. You appear to be growing more rapidly than the rest. To some this may be a curse, to you it is a gift. You gain the following benefits.

* Your hit point maximum increases by an amount equal to twice your level when you gain this feat. Whenever you gain a level thereafter, your hit point maximum increases by an additional two hit points.

* For every two base hit points you have, you weigh five pounds more.

* Your height also changes, as per the Perpetual Growth Calculator tool.

* **Big Gobbo.** If your base weight is at least 120 pounds and your base height is at least 5 feet tall, you are Medium sized.
:: *If your strength is not at least 14 at any point, your movement speed is reduced by 10 feet*

* **Large Gobbo.** If your base weight is at least 600 pounds and your base height is at least 8 feet tall, you are Large sized.
:: *If your strength is not at least 19 at any point, your movement speed is reduced by 15 feet*

* **Huge Gobbo.** If your base weight is at least 200 lbs and your base height is at least 130 feet tall
:: *If your strength is not at least 24, you are incapacitated.*

* **Gargantuan Gobbo?** If your base weight is at least lbs and your base height is at least, you are Gargantuan sized.
:: *If your strength is not at least 30 at any point, you die.*

* You may still take feats that require you to be a particular size, regardless of your current size.
