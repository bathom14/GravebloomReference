**Prerequisites:** [[Half Elf]]

You develop your magnetic personality to ease your way through the world. You gain the following benefits:

* Increase your Charisma score by 1, up to a maximum of 20.

* You gain proficiency in Deception and Persuasion skills. If you're already proficient in either skill, your proficiency bonus is doubled for any check you make with that skill.

> ### Special Traits
> ***False Appearance.*** While the creature remains motionless, it is indistinguishable from a normal mushroom.
>
> ***Spore Bond.*** During a short rest, the fungoid can form a bond with a willing humanoid it is touching, making it its spore mate. Whenever the spore mate hits with any weaapon and deals damage, that weapon deals an extra 1 radiant damage.as long as the fungoid is within 30 feet of it.
>
> ***Enduring Hyphae.*** If the common fungoid dies, it may resurrect at the location of its spore mate after 10 minutes. This trait may only be used once per day.
>
> ***Silent Speech.*** The common fungoid can communicate telepathically to any creature within 30 feet of itself that it is aware of. The recieving creature understands the meaning of the communcation if it is capable of reasoning at that level. This is a one way communication, but the recieving creature may respond back by other means. It does not inherently know where the telepathic speech is coming from. 
>
> ***Mycelial Resilience*** The fungoid regains 1 hit point at the start of its turn
>
> ### Actions
> ***Truffle Shot.*** Ranged Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, range 15/30 ft., one target. *Hit* 1 radiant damage.
>
> ***Fungal Connection (1/day).*** The next time a creature the fungoid is touching attempts to forage using the Natural Mycologist trait, it does so with advantage. It rolls two sets of d4, and recieves the higher set of rolls.
