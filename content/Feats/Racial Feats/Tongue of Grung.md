**Prerequisites:** [[Grung]]

Only a select few Grung are blessed with a viscoelastic tongue. You gain the following abilities.

* You can use your muscular tongue as a natural weapon. This weapon uses your strength modifier and does 1d6 bludgeoning damage and has reach

* When you grapple a creature you can instead wrap your tongue around it, freeing up your hands to perform other tasks

* You can, as an action on your turn, launch your tongue out at a creature within 15 feet of you. The creature must make a Dexterity (Acrobatics) check contested by your Strength (Athletics) check or be pulled 10 feet towards you and be grappled by you.
