**Prerequisites:** 4th level, [[Hadozee]]

You have obtained masterful control over all four of your limbs. You gain the following benefits.

* **All Hands.**  You may use your feet to perform any action you may normally perform with your hands. While doing so, the laws of physics still apply. If you are standing on the ground, attempting to use both hands in this way will result in you immediately falling prone. You may attempt to use one foot while standing, but your movespeed is reduced to 0 while doing so. In addition, you must pass a DC 15 Dexterity (Acrobatics) saving throw or immediately be knocked prone each time you attempt to perform an action while standing on one foot. 
  - You may support yourself in other ways to avoid this saving throw, such as balancing yourself with one of your arms or bracing yourself against a wall.

* **No Feet.**  While gliding, you do not need your feet to support yourself and are able to use them freely. In addition, when you enter a glide you may immediately draw a weapon without the Heavy or Two-Handed property from your from your body into each one of your hands. 

* **Four Weapon Fighting.**  When you take the Attack Action and Attack with a weapon that you’re holding in one hand, you can use a Bonus Action to Attack with up to 3 different  weapons that you’re holding in other hands. You don’t add your ability modifier to the damage of the bonus Attack, unless that modifier is negative. These additional attacks are taken at disadvantage.
