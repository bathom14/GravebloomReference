**Prerequisites:** 8th level, [[Wood Elf]]

The sylvan traced their origin to the Ti'ir Na Vyl. You awaken trains associated with the fae, and recieve the following benefits.

* **Decieve.** You know the [Hallucinatory Terrain](http://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:hallucinatory-terrain) spell. You may cast this spell without expending a spell slot a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, and you regain all spent uses when you complete a long rest.

* **Commune.** You know the [Commune with Nature](http://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:commune-with-nature) spell. You may cast this spell without expending a spell slot a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, and your regain all spent uses whhen you complete a long rest.

* **Endure.** You know the [Druid Grove](http://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:druid-grove) spell. You may cast this spell without expending a spell slot once per long rest. Wisodm is your spellcasting modifier for this spell. When cast this way, it has the following additonal druid grove effect.
   - **Seed of Rebirth.** If you die while this druid grove is active, you reincarnate. As long as you are on the same plane as your druid grove, you gain the effects of the [Reincarnate](http://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:reincarnate) spell upon death, creating the new body in the center of your druid grove. Your druid grove is destroyed in the process.
